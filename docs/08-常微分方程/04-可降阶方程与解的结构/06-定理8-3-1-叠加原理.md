## 定理8.3.1 叠加原理

若 $y_{1}(x)$ 和 $y_{2}(x)$ 是二阶齐线性微分方程

$$
\begin{equation*}
y^{\prime \prime}+p(x) y^{\prime}+q(x) y=0 \tag{2}
\end{equation*}
$$

的解，则它们的线性组合

$$
c_{1} y_{1}(x)+c_{2} y_{2}(x)
$$

也是方程（2）的解，
其中 $c_{1} 、 c_{2}$ 为任意常数（不一定相互独立）。

证 令 $y(x)=c_{1} y_{1}(x)+c_{2} y_{2}(x)$ ，代入方程（2）中，得

$$
\begin{aligned}
\left(c_{1} y_{1}(x)\right. & \left.+c_{2} y_{2}(x)\right)^{\prime \prime}+p(x)\left(c_{1} y_{1}(x)+c_{2} y_{2}(x)\right)^{\prime} \\
& +q(x)\left(c_{1} y_{1}(x)+c_{2} y_{2}(x)\right) \\
= & \left(c_{1} y_{1}^{\prime \prime}(x)+c_{2} y_{2}^{\prime \prime}(x)\right)+p(x)\left(c_{1} y_{1}^{\prime}(x)+c_{2} y_{2}^{\prime}(x)\right) \\
+ & q(x)\left(c_{1} y_{1}(x)+c_{2} y_{2}(x)\right) \\
= & c_{1}\left(y_{1}^{\prime \prime}(x)+p(x) y_{1}^{\prime}(x)+q(x) y_{1}(x)\right) \\
+ & c_{2}\left(y_{2}^{\prime \prime}(x)+p(x) y_{2}^{\prime}(x)+q(x) y_{2}(x)\right) \\
= & 0+0=0
\end{aligned}
$$

即 $y(x)=c_{1} y_{1}(x)+c_{2} y_{2}(x)$ 为方程（2）的解。
