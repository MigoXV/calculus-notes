## 5.场论初步

梯度 $\quad$ gradu $=\frac{\partial \boldsymbol{u}}{\partial \boldsymbol{x}} \overrightarrow{\boldsymbol{i}}+\frac{\partial \boldsymbol{u}}{\partial \boldsymbol{y}} \overrightarrow{\boldsymbol{j}}+\frac{\partial \boldsymbol{u}}{\partial \boldsymbol{z}} \overrightarrow{\boldsymbol{k}}$
通量 $\quad \Phi=\iint_{\Sigma} P d y d z+Q d z d x+R d x d y$
散度 $\quad \operatorname{div} \vec{A}=\frac{\partial P}{\partial x}+\frac{\partial Q}{\partial y}+\frac{\partial R}{\partial z}$
环流量 $\quad \Gamma=\int_{\Gamma} P d x+Q d y+R d z$
旋度 $\operatorname{rot} \vec{A}=\left(\frac{\partial R}{\partial y}-\frac{\partial Q}{\partial z}\right) \vec{i}+\left(\frac{\partial P}{\partial z}-\frac{\partial R}{\partial x}\right) \vec{j}+\left(\frac{\partial Q}{\partial x}-\frac{\partial P}{\partial y}\right) \vec{k}$
