## 例题

例1 计算 $\iiint_{\Omega}(x+z) \mathrm{d}$, 其中 $\Omega$ 由 $z=\sqrt{x^{2}+y^{2}}$ 与 $z=1+\sqrt{1-x^{2}-y^{2}}$ 所围成的．（画图）
解 $\because \Omega$ 关于 $y o z$ 面为对称，$f(x, y, z)=x$ 为 $x$ 的奇函数，有 $\iiint_{\Omega} x \mathrm{~d} v=0$ ．
$\therefore \iiint_{\Omega}(x+z) \mathrm{d} v=\iiint_{\Omega} z \mathrm{~d} v$ 利用球面坐标 $=\int_{0}^{2 \pi} \mathrm{~d} \theta \int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{4}} \mathrm{~d} \varphi \int_{0}^{2 \cos \varphi} r \cos \varphi \cdot r^{2} \sin \varphi \mathrm{~d} r=\frac{7 \pi}{6}$.

例3：计算三重积分

$$
I=\int_{0}^{1} d x \int_{0}^{1-x} d z \int_{0}^{1-x-z}(1-y) e^{-(1-y-z)^{2}} d y .
$$

直接积分困难，考虑改变积分次序

$$
\begin{aligned}
I & =\iint_{D_{y z}} \mathrm{~d} y \mathrm{~d} z \int_{0}^{1-y-z}(1-y) e^{-(1-y-z)^{2}} \mathrm{~d} x \\
& =\int_{0}^{1} \mathrm{~d} y \int_{0}^{1-y} \mathrm{~d} z \int_{0}^{1-y-z}(1-y) e^{-(1-y-z)^{2}} \mathrm{~d} x \\
1 & =\int_{0}^{1} \mathrm{~d} y \int_{0}^{1-y}(1-y)(1-y-z) e^{-(1-y-z)^{2}} \mathrm{~d} z \\
& =\int_{0}^{1} \frac{1-y}{2} \cdot\left(1-e^{-(1-y)^{2}}\right) \mathrm{d} y=\frac{1}{4 e}
\end{aligned}
$$

例4 计算 $\iiint_{\Omega}\left(z^{2}+x\right) \mathrm{d} v, \Omega: x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2} \leq R$ 与
$x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2} \leq 2 R z$ 所围的公共部分．
解 $\because \Omega$ 关于 $y o z$ 面为对称， $\iiint_{\Omega} x \mathrm{~d} v=0$ ．

$$
\begin{aligned}
I & =\iiint_{\Omega} z^{2} \mathrm{~d} v \\
= & \int_{0}^{\frac{R}{2}} z^{2} \mathrm{~d} z \iint_{D_{z}: x^{2}+y^{2} \leq 2 R z-z^{2}} \mathrm{~d} x \mathrm{~d} y+\int_{\frac{R}{2}}^{R} z^{2} \mathrm{~d} z \iint_{D_{z}: x^{2}+y^{2} \leq R^{2}-z^{2}} \mathrm{~d} x \mathrm{~d} y \\
& =\frac{59}{480} \pi R^{5}
\end{aligned}
$$

例5 计算 $\iiint_{\Omega} e^{|z|} \mathrm{d} v, \quad \Omega: x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2} \leq 1$ ．
解 ∵ 被积函数仅为 $z$ 的函数，截面 $D(z)$ 为圆域 $x^{2}+y^{2} \leq 1-z^{2}$ ，故采用＂先二后一＂法。

$$
\begin{aligned}
\iiint_{\Omega} e^{|z|} \mathrm{d} v & =2 \iiint_{\Omega_{ \pm}} e^{z} \mathrm{~d} v \\
& =2 \int_{0}^{1}\left[\iint_{D(z)} \mathrm{d} x \mathrm{~d} y\right] e^{z} \mathrm{~d} z \\
& =2 \int_{0}^{1} \pi\left(1-z^{2}\right) e^{z} \mathrm{~d} z=2 \pi
\end{aligned}
$$

例6 计算 $\iiint_{\Omega} z \sqrt{x^{2}+y^{2}} \mathrm{~d} v, \Omega: y=\sqrt{2 x-x^{2}}$ 与
$y=0, z=0, z=2$ 围成。
解 $\quad I=\iint_{D_{x y}} \sqrt{x^{2}+y^{2}} \mathrm{~d} x \mathrm{~d} y \int_{0}^{2} z \mathrm{~d} z$

$$
\begin{aligned}
& =2 \int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}} \mathrm{~d} \theta \int_{0}^{2 \cos \theta} r \cdot r \mathrm{~d} r \\
& =\frac{32}{9} .
\end{aligned}
$$
