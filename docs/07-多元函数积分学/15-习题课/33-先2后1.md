## 先二后一

$$
\text { 法 } \begin{aligned}
& \iiint_{\Omega}\left(x^{2}+y^{2}\right) \mathrm{d} v=\int_{0}^{8} \mathrm{~d} z \iint_{x^{2}+y} \\
\quad= & \int_{0}^{8} \mathrm{~d} z \int_{0}^{2 \pi} \mathrm{~d} \theta \int_{0}^{\sqrt{2 z}} r^{2} r \mathrm{~d} r \\
\quad= & \int_{0}^{2 \pi} \mathrm{~d} \theta \int_{0}^{8}\left(\left.\frac{r^{4}}{4}\right|_{0} ^{\sqrt{2 z}}\right) \mathrm{d} z \\
\quad= & \int_{0}^{2 \pi} \mathrm{~d} \theta \int_{0}^{8} z^{2} \mathrm{~d} z=\frac{1024}{3} \pi
\end{aligned}
$$

设 $\Omega$ 是由曲线 $\left\{\begin{array}{l}y^{2}=2 z_{\text {绕 }} z \text { 轴旋转一周而成 } \\ x=0\end{array}\right.$的曲面与平面 $z=8$ 围成的空间区域，旋转曲面方程为：$x^{2}+y^{2}=2 z$

法2 $\iiint_{\Omega}\left(x^{2}+y^{2}\right) \mathrm{d} v$ 柱面坐标 $=\int_{0}^{2 \pi} \mathrm{~d} \theta \int_{0}^{4} r d r \int_{\frac{r^{2}}{2}}^{8} r^{2} \mathrm{~d} z=\frac{1024}{3} \pi$

例8 计算 $I=\iiint_{\Omega}(x+y+z)^{2} \mathrm{~d} x \mathrm{~d} y \mathrm{~d} z$ ，其中 $\Omega$
是由抛物面 $z=x^{2}+y^{2}$ 和球面 $x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2}=2$所围成的空间闭区域。

解 $(x+y+z)^{2}$

$$
=x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2}+2(x y+y z+z x)
$$

$\because \Omega$ 关于 $x o z$ 面对称，$x y+y z$ 是 $y$ 的奇函数，
$\therefore \iiint_{\Omega}(x y+y z) \mathrm{d} v=0$ 。同理亦有 $\iiint_{\Omega} x z \mathrm{~d} v=0$.

于是 $I=\iiint_{\Omega}\left(x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2}\right) d x d y d z$在柱面坐标下：

投影区域 $D_{x y}: 0 \leq \theta \leq 2 \pi, 0 \leq r \leq 1$,

$$
\begin{aligned}
\Omega: \quad(r, \theta) \in D_{x y}, r^{2} \leq z \leq \sqrt{2-r^{2}} & \\
I & =\int_{0}^{2 \pi} \mathrm{~d} \theta \int_{0}^{1} r \mathrm{~d} r \int_{r^{2}}^{\sqrt{2-r^{2}}}\left(r^{2}+z^{2}\right) \mathrm{d} z \\
& =\frac{\pi}{60}(96 \sqrt{2}-89)
\end{aligned}
$$

例9 求半球面 $z=\sqrt{3 a^{2}-x^{2}-y^{2} \text { 与旋转拋物面 }} x^{2}+y^{2}=2 a z$ 所围成立体的表面积．

例10 求半球面 $z=\sqrt{3 a^{2}-x^{2}-y^{2}}$ 与旋转抛物面 $x^{2}+y^{2}=2 a z$ 所围成立体的表面积．

例10 求半球面 $z=\sqrt{3 a^{2}-x^{2}-y^{2}}$ 与旋转拋物面 $x^{2}+y^{2}=2 a z$ 所围成立体的表面积．$S=S_{1}+S_{2}=\frac{16}{3} \pi a^{2}$

解

$$
\begin{aligned}
S_{1} & =\iint_{D} \sqrt{1+z_{x}^{2}+z_{y}^{2}} \mathrm{~d} x \mathrm{~d} y=\iint_{D} \frac{\sqrt{3} a}{\sqrt{3 a^{2}-x^{2}-y^{2}}} \mathrm{~d} x \mathrm{~d} y \\
& =\sqrt{3} a \int_{0}^{2 \pi} \mathrm{~d} \theta \int_{0}^{\sqrt{2} a} \frac{1}{\sqrt{3 a^{2}-r^{2}}} r \mathrm{~d} r=2 \sqrt{3}(\sqrt{3}-1) \pi a^{2} \\
S_{2} & =\iint_{D} \sqrt{1+\left(\frac{x}{a}\right)^{2}+\left(\frac{y}{a}\right)^{2}} \mathrm{~d} x \mathrm{~d} y \quad\left\{\begin{array}{l}
z=\sqrt{3 a^{2}-x^{2}-y^{2}} \\
x^{2}+y^{2}=2 a z
\end{array}\right. \\
& =\frac{1}{a} \int_{0}^{2 \pi} \mathrm{~d} \theta \int_{0}^{\sqrt{2} a} \sqrt{a^{2}+r^{2}} r \mathrm{~d} r \quad \text { 即 } D:\left\{\begin{array}{l}
x^{2}+y^{2} \leq 2 a^{2} \\
z=0
\end{array}\right. \\
& =\left(2 \sqrt{3}-\frac{2}{3}\right) \pi a^{2}
\end{aligned}
$$

例11 求均匀半球体 $\Omega: x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2} \leq a^{2}, z \geq 0$ 的重心
设重心为 $(\bar{x}, \bar{y}, \bar{z})$ ，则 $\bar{x}=\bar{y}=0$

$$
\begin{aligned}
& \bar{z}=\frac{1}{V} \iiint_{\Omega} z \mathrm{~d} x \mathrm{~d} y \mathrm{~d} z \quad V=\frac{2}{3} \pi a^{3} \\
& =\frac{1}{V} \int_{0}^{2 \pi} \mathrm{~d} \theta \int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}} \mathrm{~d} \varphi \int_{0}^{a} r \cos \varphi \cdot r^{2} \sin \varphi \mathrm{~d} r \\
& =\frac{3}{8} a
\end{aligned}
$$

故重心为 $\left(0,0, \frac{3}{8} a\right)$

例12 计算极限 $I=\lim _{t \rightarrow 0^{+}} \frac{1}{\pi t^{4}} \iiint_{V} f\left(\sqrt{x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2}}\right) \mathrm{d} v$ ，其中 $V=\left\{(x, y, z) \mid x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2} \leq t^{2}\right\}, f(t)$ 具有连续导数，且 $\boldsymbol{f}(\mathbf{0})=\mathbf{0}$ ．
解： $\iiint_{V} f\left(\sqrt{x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2}}\right) \mathrm{d} \nu=\int_{0}^{2 \pi} \mathrm{~d} \theta \int_{0}^{\pi} \mathrm{d} \varphi \int_{0}^{t} f(r) \cdot r^{2} \sin \varphi \mathrm{~d} r$

$$
\begin{aligned}
& =4 \pi \int_{0}^{t} f(r) \cdot r^{2} \mathrm{~d} r \\
I=\lim _{t \rightarrow 0^{+}} \frac{4 \int_{0}^{t} f(r) r^{2} \mathrm{~d} r}{t^{4}} & =\lim _{t \rightarrow 0^{+}} \frac{4 f(t) t^{2}}{4 t^{3}}=\lim _{t \rightarrow 0^{+}} \frac{f(t)}{t} \\
& =\lim _{t \rightarrow 0^{+}} \frac{f^{\prime}(t)}{1}=f^{\prime}(0)
\end{aligned}
$$
