## 思考题

设 $r=\sqrt{x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2}}$ ，则

$$
\operatorname{div}(\operatorname{grad} r)=2 / r ; \operatorname{rot}(\operatorname{grad} r)=\overrightarrow{0} .
$$

提示： $\operatorname{grad} r=\left(\frac{x}{r}, \frac{y}{r}, \frac{z}{r}\right) \quad \frac{\partial}{\partial x}\left(\frac{x}{r}\right)=\frac{r-x \cdot x / r}{r^{2}}=\frac{r^{2}-x^{2}}{r^{3}}$ ，

$$
\frac{\partial}{\partial y}\left(\frac{y}{r}\right)=\frac{r^{2}-y^{2}}{r^{3}} \quad \frac{\partial}{\partial z}\left(\frac{z}{r}\right)=\frac{r^{2}-z^{2}}{r^{3}}
$$

三式相加即得 $\operatorname{div}(\operatorname{grad} r)$

$$
\operatorname{rot}(\operatorname{grad} r)=\left|\begin{array}{ccc}
\vec{i} & \vec{j} & \vec{k} \\
\frac{\partial}{\partial x} & \frac{\partial}{\partial y} & \frac{\partial}{\partial z} \\
\frac{x}{r} & \frac{y}{r} & \frac{z}{r}
\end{array}\right|=(0,0,0)
$$

$\square$
