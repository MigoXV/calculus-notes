## 三重积分换元法

定理设 $f(x, y, z)$ 在有界闭区域 $\Omega_{x y z}$ 闭上可积，若变换 $T: x=x(u, v, w), y=y(u, v, w), z=z(u, v, w)$ 将 $u v w$空间中的有界闭区域 $\Omega_{u v w}$ 变成 $x y z$ 空间中的有界闭区域 $\Omega_{x y z}$ ，且满足
1）$x=x(u, v, w), y=y(u, v, w), z=z(u, v, w) \in C^{1}\left(\Omega_{u v w}\right)$
2）$J(u, v, w)=\frac{\partial(x, y, z)}{\partial(u, v, w)}=\left|\begin{array}{lll}\frac{\partial x}{\partial u} & \frac{\partial x}{\partial v} & \frac{\partial x}{\partial w} \\ \frac{\partial y}{\partial u} & \frac{\partial y}{\partial v} & \frac{\partial y}{\partial w} \\ \frac{\partial z}{\partial u} & \frac{\partial z}{\partial v} & \frac{\partial z}{\partial w}\end{array}\right| \neq 0,(u, v, w) \in \Omega_{u v w}$
3）该变换 $\mathrm{T}: \Omega_{u v w} \rightarrow \Omega_{x y z}$ 是一一映射，则有

$$
\begin{aligned}
& \iiint_{\Omega_{x y z}} f(x, y, z) \mathrm{d} x \mathrm{~d} y \mathrm{~d} z \\
= & \iiint_{\Omega_{u w w}} f(x(u, v, w), y(u, v, w), z(u, v, w))|J(u, v, w)| d u d v d w
\end{aligned}
$$

若变换 T 为柱坐标：$\left\{\begin{array}{l}x=r \cos \theta \\ y=r \sin \theta \\ z=z\end{array}\right.$
其中 $0 \leq r<+\infty, \quad 0 \leq \theta \leq 2 \pi, \quad-\infty<z<+\infty$ ．

$$
\begin{aligned}
\frac{\partial(x, y, z)}{\partial(r, \theta, z)} & =\left|\begin{array}{lll}
\frac{\partial x}{\partial r} & \frac{\partial y}{\partial r} & \frac{\partial z}{\partial r} \\
\frac{\partial x}{\partial \theta} & \frac{\partial y}{\partial \theta} & \frac{\partial z}{\partial \theta} \\
\frac{\partial x}{\partial z} & \frac{\partial y}{\partial z} & \frac{\partial z}{\partial z}
\end{array}\right|=\left|\begin{array}{ccc}
\cos \theta & \sin \theta & 0 \\
-r \sin \theta & r \cos \theta & 0 \\
0 & 0 & 1
\end{array}\right| \\
& =\left|\begin{array}{cc}
\cos \theta & \sin \theta \\
-r \sin \theta & r \cos \theta
\end{array}\right|=r
\end{aligned}
$$

所以， $\iiint_{\Omega_{x y z}} f(x, y, z) \mathrm{d} x \mathrm{~d} y \mathrm{~d} z$

$$
=\iiint_{\Omega_{r \theta z}} f(r \cos \theta, r \sin \theta, z) r \mathrm{~d} r \mathrm{~d} \theta \mathrm{~d} z
$$

一般将 $\Omega_{r \theta z}$ 表为：$z_{1}(r, \theta) \leq z \leq z_{2}(r, \theta)$,

$$
\begin{aligned}
& r_{1}(\theta) \leq r \leq r_{2}(\theta), \\
& \alpha \leq \theta \leq \beta .
\end{aligned}
$$

$$
\begin{aligned}
& \iiint_{\Omega_{x y z}} f(x, y, z) \mathrm{d} x \mathrm{~d} y \mathrm{~d} z \\
& \quad=\iiint_{\Omega_{r \theta z}} f(r \cos \theta, r \sin \theta, z) r \mathrm{~d} r \mathrm{~d} \theta \mathrm{~d} z \\
& \quad=\int_{\alpha}^{\beta} d \theta \int_{r_{1}(\theta)}^{r_{2}(\theta)} r d r \int_{z_{1}(r, \theta)}^{z_{2}(r, \theta)} f(r \cos \theta, r \sin \theta, z) d z
\end{aligned}
$$

若变换 T 为球坐标：$\left\{\begin{array}{l}x=\rho \sin \varphi \cos \theta \\ y=\rho \sin \varphi \sin \theta \\ z=\rho \cos \varphi\end{array}\right.$

$$
\begin{aligned}
& \text { 由 } \\
& \frac{\partial(x, y, z)}{\partial(\rho, \varphi, \theta)}=\left|\begin{array}{lll}
\frac{\partial x}{\partial \rho} & \frac{\partial y}{\partial \rho} & \frac{\partial z}{\partial \rho} \\
\frac{\partial x}{\partial \varphi} & \frac{\partial y}{\partial \varphi} & \frac{\partial z}{\partial \varphi} \\
\frac{\partial x}{\partial \theta} & \frac{\partial y}{\partial \theta} & \frac{\partial z}{\partial \theta}
\end{array}\right|=\left|\begin{array}{ccc}
\sin \varphi \cos \theta & \sin \varphi \sin \theta & \cos \varphi \\
\rho \cos \varphi \cos \theta & \rho \cos \varphi \sin \theta & -\rho \sin \varphi \\
-\rho \sin \varphi \sin \theta & \rho \sin \varphi \cos \theta & 0
\end{array}\right| \\
& \quad=\rho^{2} \sin \varphi \sin ^{2} \theta+\rho^{2} \sin \varphi \cos ^{2} \theta=\rho^{2} \sin \varphi
\end{aligned}
$$

$$
\begin{aligned}
& \text { 所以 } I=\iiint_{\Omega_{x y z}} f(x, y, z) \mathrm{d} x \mathrm{~d} y \mathrm{~d} z \\
& =\iiint_{\Omega_{\rho \varphi \theta}} \boldsymbol{f}(\rho \boldsymbol{\operatorname { s i n }} \varphi \boldsymbol{\operatorname { c o s }} \theta, \rho \boldsymbol{\operatorname { s i n }} \varphi \boldsymbol{\operatorname { s i n }} \theta, \rho \boldsymbol{\operatorname { c o s }} \varphi) \rho^{2} \boldsymbol{\operatorname { s i n }} \varphi \mathrm{~d} \rho \mathrm{~d} \varphi \mathrm{~d} \theta
\end{aligned}
$$

积分次序通常为 $\rho \rightarrow \varphi \rightarrow \theta$ ．
若 $\Omega$ 的边界为包围原点在内 的闭曲面 $\rho=\rho(\theta, \varphi)$ ，则

$$
I=\int_{0}^{2 \pi} d \theta \int_{0}^{\pi} \sin \varphi d \varphi \int_{0}^{\rho(\theta, \varphi)} F(\rho, \varphi, \theta) \rho^{2} d \rho
$$

例9 求 $\iiint_{\Omega} d v$ ，其中 $\Omega$ 由 $z=x^{2}+y^{2}, z=\frac{1}{2}\left(x^{2}+y^{2}\right) x+y= \pm 1, x-y= \pm 1$ 围成。

解 $\Omega=\left\{(x, y, z) \left\lvert\, \frac{1}{2} \leq \frac{z}{x^{2}+y^{2}} \leq 1\right.,-1 \leq x+y \leq 1,-1 \leq x-y \leq 1\right\}$

$$
\begin{aligned}
\text { 令 } u & =x+y, v=x-y, w=\frac{z}{x^{2}+y^{2}} \\
x & =\frac{u+v}{2}, y=\frac{u-v}{2}, z=\frac{1}{2}\left(u^{2}+v^{2}\right) w \\
\Omega^{\prime} & =\left\{(u, v, w) \left\lvert\, \frac{1}{2} \leq w \leq 1\right.,-1 \leq v \leq 1,-1 \leq u \leq 1\right\}
\end{aligned}
$$

$$
\begin{aligned}
& J(u, v, w)=\left|\begin{array}{ccc}
\frac{1}{2} & \frac{1}{2} & 0 \\
\frac{1}{2} & -\frac{1}{2} & 0 \\
u w & v w & \frac{1}{2}\left(u^{2}+v^{2}\right)
\end{array}\right|=-\frac{1}{4}\left(u^{2}+v^{2}\right) \\
& \therefore \iiint_{\Omega} d v=\int_{\frac{1}{2}}^{1} d w \int_{-1}^{1} d v \int_{-1}^{1} \frac{1}{4}\left(u^{2}+v^{2}\right) d u \\
& \quad=2 \cdot \frac{1}{4} \int_{\frac{1}{2}}^{1} d w \int_{-1}^{1} d v \int_{-1}^{1} u^{2} d u=2 \cdot \frac{1}{4} \cdot \frac{1}{2} \cdot 2 \cdot \frac{2}{3}=\frac{1}{3} .
\end{aligned}
$$

例10 试计算椭球体 $\frac{x^{2}}{a^{2}}+\frac{y^{2}}{b^{2}}+\frac{z^{2}}{c^{2}} \leq 1$ 的体积 $V$ ．
解 对于椭球体 $\frac{x^{2}}{a^{2}}+\frac{y^{2}}{b^{2}}+\frac{z^{2}}{c^{2}} \leq 1$ 使用广义球坐标变换．
令 $T: x=a \rho \sin \varphi \cos \theta, y=b \rho \sin \varphi \sin \theta, z=c \rho \cos \varphi$

$$
\begin{aligned}
|J| & =a b c \rho^{2} \sin \varphi,(0 \leq \varphi \leq \pi) \\
\therefore V & =\iiint_{\Omega} d v=\iiint_{\Omega} a b c \rho^{2} \sin \varphi d \rho d \varphi d \theta \\
& =a b c \int_{0}^{2 \pi} d \theta \int_{0}^{\pi} \sin \varphi d \varphi \int_{0}^{1} \rho^{2} d \rho=\frac{4}{3} \pi a b c
\end{aligned}
$$

$$

$$

$(0<\alpha<\beta), z=h(h>0)$ 所围成的区域。
解 作变换 $\boldsymbol{T}: \boldsymbol{u}=\frac{\boldsymbol{z}}{\boldsymbol{y}^{2}}, \boldsymbol{v}=\frac{\boldsymbol{z}}{\boldsymbol{x}}, \boldsymbol{w}=\boldsymbol{z}$ ，则 $\Omega$ 变成 $\Omega *$ ：

$$
\Omega^{*}=\{(u, v, w) \mid a \leq u \leq b, \alpha \leq v \leq \beta, 0 \leq w \leq h\},

而 $\frac{\partial(x, y, z)}{\partial(u, v, w)}=\left(\frac{\partial(u, v, w)}{\partial(x, y, z)}\right)^{-1}=-\frac{x^{2} y^{3}}{2 z^{2}}=-\frac{1}{2 v^{2}}\left(\frac{w}{u}\right)^{\frac{3}{2}}$ ．

由换元积分法公式

$$
\begin{aligned}
I & =\iiint_{\Omega} x^{2} d x d y d z=\iiint_{\Omega^{*}} \frac{w^{2}}{v^{2}} \cdot \frac{1}{2 v^{2}}\left(\frac{w}{u}\right)^{\frac{3}{2}} d u d v d w \\

例12 计算曲面 $\left(x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2}\right)^{2}=a^{3} z(a>0)$所围成的立体体积 $\boldsymbol{V}$ ．

解 该曲面关于 $y z$ 平面和 $x z$ 平面对称，且位于 $x y$平面上方，故只需计算在第一卦限中 $\Omega_{1}$ 的体积 $\boldsymbol{V}_{\mathbf{1}}$ ，则

$$
V=4 V_{1}=4 \iiint_{\Omega_{1}} d x d y d z
$$

做变换 $\mathrm{T}: x=\rho \sin \varphi \cos \theta, y=\rho \sin \varphi \sin \theta, z=\rho \cos \varphi$则曲面方程为 $\quad \rho^{3}=a^{3} \cos \varphi$

且 $\frac{\partial(x, y, z)}{\partial(\rho, \varphi, \theta)}=\left|\begin{array}{lll}\frac{\partial x}{\partial \rho} & \frac{\partial y}{\partial \rho} & \frac{\partial z}{\partial \rho} \\ \frac{\partial x}{\partial \varphi} & \frac{\partial y}{\partial \varphi} & \frac{\partial z}{\partial \varphi} \\ \frac{\partial x}{\partial \theta} & \frac{\partial y}{\partial \theta} & \frac{\partial z}{\partial \theta}\end{array}\right|=\rho^{2} \sin \varphi$
而在第一卦限中， $0 \leq \theta \leq \frac{\pi}{2}, 0 \leq \varphi \leq \frac{\pi}{2}$ ，
所以曲面方程可表示为 $\rho=a \sqrt[3]{\cos \varphi}$ 。
这时 $\Omega_{1}$ 变成 $\Omega_{1}^{*}$ ：

$$
\Omega_{1}^{*}=\left\{(\rho, \varphi, \theta) \left\lvert\, 0 \leq \theta \leq \frac{\pi}{2}\right., 0 \leq \varphi \leq \frac{\pi}{2}, 0 \leq \rho \leq a \sqrt[3]{\cos \varphi}\right\},
$$

故 $V=4 V_{1}=4 \iiint_{\Omega_{1}} \rho^{2} \sin \varphi d \rho d \varphi d \theta$

$$
\begin{aligned}
& =4 \int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}} d \theta \int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}} \sin \varphi d \varphi \int_{0}^{a \sqrt[3]{\cos \varphi}} \rho^{2} d \rho \\
& =\frac{4 \pi}{6} a^{3} \int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}} \sin \varphi \cos \varphi d \varphi=\frac{1}{3} \pi a^{3} .
\end{aligned}
$$

$2^{\circ}$ 在什么样的情况下应作怎样的替换，应从被积函数特点和区域形状两方面来考虑，通过练习小结一些方法。

$$

& =\int_{0}^{1} d u \int_{0}^{u} \frac{v}{u} \cdot e^{u^{2}} d v=\int_{0}^{1} \frac{u}{2} \cdot e^{u^{2}} d u=\frac{1}{4}(e-1) .
\end{aligned}
$$
