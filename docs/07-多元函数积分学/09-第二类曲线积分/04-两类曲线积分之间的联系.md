## 五、两类曲线积分之间的联系

1．设有向平面曲线弧为 $L:\left\{\begin{array}{l}x=\varphi(s) \\ y=\psi(s)\end{array}, 0 \leq s \leq l\right.$
$L$ 上点 $(x, y)$ 处的切线向量 $\vec{T}=\left\{x_{s}^{\prime}, y_{s}^{\prime}\right\}$ 的方向角为 $\alpha, \beta$ ，

$$
\left.\left.\left.\begin{array}{ll}
\left(\cos \alpha=\frac{x_{s}^{\prime}}{\sqrt{x_{s}^{\prime 2}+y_{s}^{\prime 2}}}, \cos \beta=\frac{y_{s}^{\prime}}{\sqrt{x_{s}^{\prime 2}+y_{s}^{\prime 2}}}\right) & \cos \alpha=\frac{\mathrm{d} x}{\mathrm{~d} s}, \cos \beta=\frac{\mathrm{d} y}{\mathrm{~d} s} \\
\text { 则 } \int_{L} P d x+Q d y=\int_{L}\left(P \frac{d x}{d s}+Q \frac{d y}{d s}\right) d s & d s=\sqrt{\left.\varphi^{\prime}(s)\right]^{2}+\left[\psi^{\prime}(s)\right]^{2}} d s \\
=\int_{L}\left\{P(\varphi(s), \psi(s)) \frac{\varphi^{\prime}(s)}{\sqrt{\left[\varphi^{\prime}(s)\right]^{2}+\left[\psi^{\prime}(s)\right]^{2}}}=1\right. & \\
= & \int_{L}\left[P(\varphi(\varphi)]^{2}+\left[\psi^{\prime}(s)\right]^{2}\right.
\end{array} Q(\phi(s), \psi(s)) \frac{\psi^{\prime}(s)}{\sqrt{\left[\varphi^{\prime}(s)\right]^{2}+\left[\psi^{\prime}(s)\right]^{2}}}\right\} d s\right) \cos \alpha+Q(\varphi(s), \psi(s)) \cos \beta\right] d s . ~ l_{n} d s
$$

2．设有向平面曲线弧为 $L:\left\{\begin{array}{l}x=\varphi(t) \\ y=\psi(t)\end{array}, 0 \leq t \leq l\right.$

$$
d x=x_{t}^{\prime} d t, d y=y_{t}^{\prime} d t, d s=\sqrt{x_{t}^{\prime 2}+y_{t}^{\prime 2}} d t
$$

$L$ 上点 $(x, y)$ 处的切线向量 $\vec{T}=\left\{x_{t}^{\prime}, y_{t}^{\prime}\right\}$ 的方向角为 $\alpha, \beta$ ，

$$
\left(\cos \alpha=\frac{x_{t}^{\prime}}{\sqrt{x_{t}^{\prime 2}+y_{t}^{\prime 2}}}=\frac{d x}{d s}, \cos \beta=\frac{y_{t}^{\prime}}{\sqrt{x_{t}^{\prime 2}+y_{t}^{\prime 2}}}=\frac{d y}{d s}\right)
$$

$$
\text { 则 } \int_{L} P d x+Q d y=\int_{L}\left(P \frac{d x}{d s}+Q \frac{d y}{d s}\right) d s
$$

$$
\begin{aligned}
& =\int_{L}\left\{P(\varphi(t), \psi(t)) \frac{\varphi^{\prime}(t)}{\sqrt{\left[\varphi^{\prime}(t)\right]^{2}+\left[\psi^{\prime}(t)\right]^{2}}}+Q(\varphi(t), \psi(s)) \frac{\psi^{\prime}(t)}{\sqrt{\left[\varphi^{\prime}(t)\right]^{2}+\left[\psi^{\prime}(t)\right]^{2}}}\right\} d s \\
& =\int_{L}[P(\varphi(t), \psi(t)) \cos \alpha+Q(\varphi(t), \psi(t)) \cos \beta] d s
\end{aligned}
$$

$$
\therefore \int_{L} P d x+Q d y=\int_{L}(P \cos \alpha+Q \cos \beta) d s
$$

其中 $\cos \alpha, \cos \beta$ 是 $L$ 在 $(x, y)$ 处的切向量的方向余弦。

同理，对空间第一类曲线积分与第二类曲线积分的关系为

$$
\int_{L} P d x+Q d y+R d z=\int_{L}(P \cos \alpha+Q \cos \beta+R \cos \gamma) d s
$$

$\cos \alpha, \cos \beta, \cos \gamma$ 是 $\Gamma$ 在 $(x, y, z)$ 处的切向量的方向余弦。

例9 将 $\int_{L} P(x, y) d x+Q(x, y) d y$ 化为对弧长的曲线积分，其中 $L$ 是上半圆周 $x^{2}+y^{2}=2 x$ 从 $(0,0)$ 到 $(1,1)$ ．

例10 设 $\Gamma$ 为曲线 $x=t, y=t^{2}, z=t^{3}$ 相应于 $t$ 从 0 变到 1 的曲线弧，把对坐标的曲线积分 $\int_{\Gamma} P \mathrm{~d} x+Q \mathrm{~d} y+R \mathrm{~d} z$化成对弧长的曲线积分．

例9 将 $\int_{L} P(x, y) d x+Q(x, y) d y$ 化为对弧长的曲线积分，其中 $L$ 是上半圆周 $x^{2}+y^{2}=2 x$ 从 $(0,0)$ 到 $(1,1)$ ．

解 依题意，$L$ 为 $y=\sqrt{2 x-x^{2}}$ ．

$$
\begin{aligned}
& \vec{T}=\left\{\frac{d x}{d x}, \frac{d y}{d x}\right\}=\left\{1, \frac{1-x}{\sqrt{2 x-x^{2}}}\right\} \\
& \cos \alpha=\sqrt{2 x-x^{2}}, \quad \cos \beta=1-x \\
& \therefore \int_{L} P(x, y) d x+Q(x, y) d y \\
& =\int_{L}\left[P(x, y) \sqrt{2 x-x^{2}}+Q(x, y)(1-x)\right] d s
\end{aligned}
$$

例10 设 $\Gamma$ 为曲线 $x=t, y=t^{2}, z=t^{3}$ 相应于 $t$ 从 0 变到 1 的曲线弧，把对坐标的曲线积分 $\int_{\Gamma} P \mathrm{~d} x+Q \mathrm{~d} y+R \mathrm{~d} z$化成对弧长的曲线积分．

解 曲线在任意点 $(x, y, z)$ 的切向量为 $\vec{T}=\left\{1,2 t, 3 t^{2}\right\}$

$$
\begin{array}{rlr}
\cos \alpha & =\frac{1}{\sqrt{1+2 x^{2}+9 x^{4}}}, \quad & =\left\{1,2 x, 3 x^{2}\right\} \\
\cos \beta & =\frac{2 x}{\sqrt{1+2 x^{2}+9 x^{4}}}, \quad \cos \gamma=\frac{3 x^{2}}{\sqrt{1+2 x^{2}+9 x^{4}}} \\
\therefore \int_{\Gamma} P d x+Q d y+R d z=\int_{\Gamma} \frac{P+2 x Q+3 x^{2} R}{\sqrt{1+2 x^{2}+9 x^{4}}} d s
\end{array}
$$
