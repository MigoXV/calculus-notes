## （一）求定义域和极限

1．求 $f(x, y, z)=\arcsin \frac{x^{2}+y^{2}}{z}$ 的定义域。
2．讨论极限
（1） $\lim _{\substack{x \rightarrow 0 \\ y \rightarrow 0}}=\frac{1-\cos (x+y)}{(x+y)^{2}}$ ；
（2） $\lim _{\substack{x \rightarrow 0 \\ y \rightarrow 0}} \frac{x y}{x^{2}+y^{2}}$

参考答案：
1．$(z>0) z \geq x^{2}+y^{2}$ 或 $(z<0), z \leq-x^{2}-y^{2}$
2．（1）令 $u=x+y, \quad \frac{1}{2}$ 。
（2）设沿直线 $y=k x$ 趋近于 $(0,0)$

$$
\lim _{\substack{x \rightarrow 0 \\ y=k x}} \frac{x y}{x^{2}+y^{2}}=\lim _{x \rightarrow 0} \frac{k x^{2}}{\left(1+k^{2}\right) x^{2}}=\frac{k}{1+k^{2}}
$$

故极限不存在．
（二）求偏导数和全微分：
1．求 $u=x^{y+z^{2}}$ 的一阶偏导数及全微分．
2．设 $z=\ln \left(x^{2}+\sin y\right)$ ，求 $\frac{\partial^{2} z}{\partial x \partial y}$ ．
3．$z(x, y)=x^{2} e^{y}+(x-1) \arcsin \frac{y}{x}$求 $z_{x}^{\prime}(1,0)$ ．

## 参考答案

1．求 $u=x^{y+z^{2}}$ 的一阶偏导数及全微分．
解 $\quad \frac{\partial u}{\partial x}=\left(y+z^{2}\right) x^{y+z^{2}-1} \quad \frac{\partial u}{\partial y}=x^{y+z^{2}} \ln x$ ，

$$
\frac{\partial u}{\partial z}=2 z x^{y+z^{2}} \ln x
$$

$$
\begin{aligned}
d u= & \left(y+z^{2}\right) x^{y+z^{2}-1} d x+x^{y+z^{2}} \ln x d y \\
& +2 z x^{y+z^{2}} \ln x d z
\end{aligned}
$$

2．设 $z=\ln \left(x^{2}+\sin y\right)$ ，求 $\frac{\partial^{2} z}{\partial x \partial y}$ ．

$$
\text { 解 } \begin{aligned}
\frac{\partial z}{\partial x} & =\frac{2 x}{x^{2}+\sin y}, \\
\frac{\partial^{2} z}{\partial x \partial y} & =-\frac{2 x \cos y}{\left(x^{2}+\sin y\right)^{2}} .
\end{aligned}
$$

3．$z(x, y)=x^{2} e^{y}+(x-1) \arcsin \frac{y}{x}$ 求 $z_{x}(1,0)$ ．
解 $z_{x}(1,0)=\left[\frac{d}{d x} z(x, 0)\right]_{x=1}=\left.\left(x^{2}\right)^{\prime}\right|_{x=1}=2$
若求 $z_{y}(1,0)$ ，则

$$
z_{y}(1,0)=\left[\frac{d}{d y} z(1, y)\right]_{y=0}=\left.\left(e^{y}\right)^{\prime}\right|_{y=0}=1
$$

4．$z=f\left(x, \frac{x}{y}\right), f$ 二阶偏导连续，求 $\frac{\partial z}{\partial x}, \frac{\partial z}{\partial y}, \frac{\partial^{2} z}{\partial x \partial y}$ ．
5．$z=e^{x^{2}+y^{2}}, y=\cos x$ ，求 $\frac{d z}{d x}$ 。
6．$\frac{x}{z}=\ln \frac{z}{y}$ 求 $\frac{\partial z}{\partial x}, \frac{\partial^{2} z}{\partial x^{2}}$ ．
7．设 $F(x, y)$ 具有连续偏导数，已知方程 $F\left(\frac{x}{z}, \frac{y}{z}\right)=0$ ，求 $\mathrm{d} z$ 。

4．$z=f\left(x, \frac{x}{y}\right), f$ 二阶偏导连续，求 $\frac{\partial z}{\partial x}, \frac{\partial z}{\partial y}, \frac{\partial^{2} z}{\partial x \partial y}$ ．
解 $\frac{\partial z}{\partial x}=f_{1}^{\prime}+\frac{1}{y} f_{2}^{\prime}, \frac{\partial z}{\partial y}=-\frac{x}{y^{2}} f_{2}^{\prime}$ ，

$$
\begin{gathered}
\frac{\partial^{2} z}{\partial x \partial y}=\frac{\partial}{\partial y}\left(f_{1}^{\prime}+\frac{1}{y} f_{2}^{\prime}\right)=\frac{\partial f_{1}^{\prime}}{\partial y}-\frac{1}{y^{2}} f_{2}^{\prime}+\frac{1}{y} \frac{\partial f_{2}^{\prime}}{\partial y} \\
\frac{\partial f_{1}^{\prime}}{\partial y}=-\frac{x}{y^{2}} f_{12}^{\prime}, \frac{\partial f_{2}^{\prime}}{\partial y}=-\frac{x}{y^{2}} f_{22}^{\prime} \\
\therefore \frac{\partial^{2} z}{\partial x \partial y}=-\frac{x}{y^{2}} f_{12}^{\prime \prime}-\frac{x}{y^{3}} f_{22}^{\prime \prime}-\frac{1}{y^{2}} f_{2}^{\prime} .
\end{gathered}
$$

5．$z=e^{x^{2}+y^{2}}, y=\cos x$ ，求 $\frac{d z}{d x}$ 。
解 $\frac{d z}{d x}=(2 x-\sin 2 x) e^{x^{2}+\cos ^{2} x}$
6. $\frac{x}{z}=\ln \frac{z}{y}$ 求 $\frac{\partial z}{\partial x}, \frac{\partial^{2} z}{\partial x^{2}}$.

解 $\frac{\partial z}{\partial x}=\frac{z}{x+z}$,

$$
\frac{\partial^{2} z}{\partial x^{2}}=\frac{\frac{\partial z}{\partial x}(x+z)-z\left(1+\frac{\partial z}{\partial x}\right)}{(x+z)^{2}}=\frac{z^{2}}{(x+z)^{3}} .
$$

7．设 $F(x, y)$ 具有连续偏导数，已知方程 $F\left(\frac{x}{z}, \frac{y}{z}\right)=0$ ，求 $\mathbf{d} \boldsymbol{z}$ 。

解法1利用隐函数偏导数公式
设 $z=f(x, y)$ 是由方程 $F\left(\frac{x}{z}, \frac{y}{z}\right)=0$ 确定的隐函数，
则 $\frac{\partial z}{\partial x}=-\frac{F_{1}^{\prime} \cdot \frac{1}{z} \quad z \quad z}{F_{1}^{\prime} \cdot\left(-\frac{x}{z^{2}}\right)+F_{2}^{\prime} \cdot\left(-\frac{y}{z^{2}}\right)}=\frac{z F_{1}^{\prime}}{x F_{1}^{\prime}+y F_{2}^{\prime}}$

$$
\frac{\partial z}{\partial y}=-\frac{F_{2}^{\prime} \cdot \frac{1}{z}}{F_{1}^{\prime} \cdot\left(-\frac{x}{z^{2}}\right)+F_{2}^{\prime} \cdot\left(-\frac{y}{z^{2}}\right)}=\frac{z F_{2}^{\prime}}{x F_{1}^{\prime}+y F_{2}^{\prime}}
$$

故 $\mathrm{d} z=\frac{\partial z}{\partial x} \mathrm{~d} x+\frac{\partial z}{\partial y} \mathrm{~d} y=\frac{z}{x F_{1}^{\prime}+y F_{2}^{\prime}}\left(F_{1}^{\prime} \mathrm{d} x+F_{2}^{\prime} \mathrm{d} y\right)$


## 解法2 用全微分形式不变性

对方程 $F\left(\frac{x}{z}, \frac{y}{z}\right)=0$ 两边求全微分，得

$$
\begin{aligned}
& d F\left(\frac{x}{z}, \frac{y}{z}\right)=0 \quad \text { 即 } F_{1}^{\prime} \cdot d\left(\frac{x}{z}\right)+F_{2}^{\prime} \cdot d\left(\frac{y}{z}\right)=0 \\
& F_{1}^{\prime} \cdot\left(\frac{1}{z} d x-\frac{x}{z^{2}} d z\right)+F_{2}^{\prime} \cdot\left(\frac{1}{z} d y-\frac{y}{z^{2}} d z\right)=0 \\
& F_{1}^{\prime} \cdot \frac{1}{z} d x+F_{2}^{\prime} \cdot \frac{1}{z} d y-\left(F_{1}^{\prime} \cdot \frac{x}{z^{2}}+F_{2}^{\prime} \cdot \frac{y}{z^{2}}\right) d z=0 \\
& \therefore d z=\frac{z}{x F_{1}^{\prime}+y F_{2}^{\prime}}\left(F_{1}^{\prime} \mathrm{d} x+F_{2}^{\prime} \mathrm{d} y\right)
\end{aligned}
$$

（三）曲线的切线和法平面、曲面的切平面和法线
1．求曲线 $x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2}=6, x+y+z=0$
在点 $P_{0}(1,-2,1)$ 处的切线方程及法平面方程．

2．作一平面与直线 $\left\{\begin{array}{c}x-y+z=0 \\ 2 x-y+3 z-2=0\end{array}\right.$ 垂直且与球面 $x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2}=4$ 相切．

## 参考答案

1．求曲线 $x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2}=6, x+y+z=0$
在点 $P_{0}(1,-2,1)$ 处的切线方程及法平面方程．
解 方程组 $\left\{\begin{array}{c}x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2}=6 \\ x+y+z=0\end{array}\right.$ 确定隐函数
即曲线 $y=y(x), z=z(x)$
其切向量为 $\vec{T}=\left.\left(1, y^{\prime}(x), z^{\prime}(x)\right)\right|_{P_{0}}=(1,0,-1)$
切线方程：$\frac{x-1}{1}=\frac{y+2}{0}=\frac{z-1}{-1}$
法平面方程：$(x-1)-(z-1)=0$

2．作一平面与直线 $\left\{\begin{array}{c}x-y+z=0 \\ 2 x-y+3 z-2=0\end{array}\right.$ 垂直且与球面 $x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2}=4$ 相切．

解 设切点为 $P_{0}\left(x_{0}, y_{0}, z_{0}\right)$ ，
所求平面的法向量

$$
\vec{n}_{1}=\left|\begin{array}{ccc}
\vec{i} & \vec{j} & \vec{k} \\
1 & -1 & 1 \\
2 & -1 & 3
\end{array}\right|=-2 \vec{i}-\vec{j}+\vec{k}
$$

方法 1 所求平面设为 $2 x+y-z+D=0$

与球面 $x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2}=4$ 相切．
又所求平面为 $2 x+y-z+D=0$
由（切）点到原点的距离公式，有

$$
\frac{\left|0 \cdot x_{0}+0 \cdot y_{0}+0 \cdot z_{0}+D\right|}{\sqrt{2^{2}+1^{2}+1^{2}}}=2,
$$

即 $|\boldsymbol{D}|=\mathbf{\pm 2} \sqrt{\mathbf{6}}$
所求平面： $2 x+y-z \pm 2 \sqrt{6}=0$

2．作一平面与直线 $\left\{\begin{array}{c}x-y+z=0 \\ 2 x-y+3 z-2=0\end{array}\right.$ 垂直且与球面 $x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2}=4$ 相切．

方法2 所求平面的法向量 $\vec{n}_{1}=-2 \vec{i}-\vec{j}+\vec{k}$则球面的法向量为：

$$
\begin{aligned}
& \vec{n}=\left.\left\{F_{x}, F_{y}, F_{z}\right\}\right|_{P_{0}}=\left\{2 x_{0}, 2 y_{0}, 2 z_{0}\right\} \\
& \because \vec{n} / / \vec{n}_{1}, \quad \therefore \frac{x_{0}}{2}=\frac{y_{0}}{1}=\frac{z_{0}}{-1}=t
\end{aligned}
$$

$$
\begin{aligned}
& \because \vec{n} / / \vec{n}_{1}, \quad \therefore \frac{x_{0}}{2}=\frac{y_{0}}{1}=\frac{z_{0}}{-1}=t, \\
& \text { 即 } x_{0}=2 t, y_{0}=t, z_{0}=-1,
\end{aligned}
$$

代入曲面，得

$$
t=\frac{ \pm 2}{\sqrt{6}}, x_{0}=\frac{ \pm 4}{\sqrt{6}}, y_{0}=\frac{ \pm 2}{\sqrt{6}}, z_{0}=\frac{\mp 2}{\sqrt{6}},
$$

所求方程为 $2\left(x-\frac{ \pm 4}{\sqrt{6}}\right)+\left(y-\frac{ \pm 2}{\sqrt{6}}\right)-\left(z-\frac{\mp 2}{\sqrt{6}}\right)=0$即 $2 x+y-z \pm 2 \sqrt{6}=0$
