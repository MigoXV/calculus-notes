## 1.空间曲线的切线及法平面

（1）设空间曲线：
$x=x(t), y=y(t), z=z(t) \quad$（ $t$ 为参数）
$M_{0}\left(x_{0}, y_{0}, z_{0}\right)$ 是曲线上一点，其相应的参数为 $t_{0}$ ，
则曲线在点 $M_{0}$ 处切向量为

$$
\vec{T}=\left\{x^{\prime}\left(t_{0}\right), y^{\prime}\left(t_{0}\right) z^{\prime}\left(t_{0}\right)\right\},
$$

曲线在点 $M_{0}$ 处的切线方程为

$$
\frac{x-x_{0}}{x^{\prime}\left(t_{0}\right)}=\frac{y-y_{0}}{y^{\prime}\left(t_{0}\right)}=\frac{z-z_{0}}{z^{\prime}\left(t_{0}\right)}
$$

曲线在点 $M_{0}$ 处的法平面方程为

$$
x^{\prime}\left(t_{0}\right)\left(x-x_{0}\right)+y^{\prime}\left(t_{0}\right)\left(y-y_{0}\right)+z^{\prime}\left(t_{0}\right)\left(z-z_{0}\right)=0
$$

若曲线的方程表示为

$$
\left\{\begin{array} { l } 
{ y = y ( x ) } \\
{ z = z ( x ) }
\end{array} \Leftarrow \left\{\begin{array}{l}
F(x, y, z)=0 \\
G(x, y, z)=0
\end{array}\right.\right.
$$

则在点 $M_{0}$ 处切向量为

$$
\vec{T}=\left\{1, y^{\prime}\left(x_{0}\right) z^{\prime}\left(x_{0}\right)\right\}
$$

## 2.曲面的切平面及法线

（1）设曲面方程为（隐函数形式）

$$
F(x, y, z)=0
$$

$M_{0}\left(x_{0}, y_{0}, z_{0}\right)$ 为曲面上一点，则曲面在点 $M_{0}$ 处的法向量为

$$
\vec{n}=\left(F_{x}^{\prime}, F_{y}^{\prime}, F_{z}^{\prime}\right)_{M_{0}}
$$

## 切平面方程为

$$
\begin{aligned}
& F_{x}^{\prime}\left(x_{0}, y_{0}, z_{0}\right)\left(x-x_{0}\right)+F_{y}^{\prime}\left(x_{0}, y_{0}, z_{0}\right)\left(y-y_{0}\right) \\
& +F_{z}^{\prime}\left(x_{0}, y_{0}, z_{0}\right)\left(z-z_{0}\right)=0
\end{aligned}
$$

## 法线方程为

$$
\frac{x-x_{0}}{F_{x}^{\prime}\left(x_{0}, y_{0}, z_{0}\right)}=\frac{y-y_{0}}{F_{y}^{\prime}\left(x_{0}, y_{0}, z_{0}\right)}=\frac{z-z_{0}}{F_{z}^{\prime}\left(x_{0}, y_{0}, z_{0}\right)}
$$

（2）若曲面方程为（显函数形式）

$$
z=f(x, y)
$$

$\begin{array}{ll}\text { 则可写为隐函数形式 } & F(x, y, z)=0 \\ f(x, y)-z=0 & \text {（或 } \\ f-f(x, y)=0 \text { ）}\end{array}$
曲面上 $M_{0}$ 点的法向量为

$$
\vec{n}=\left(f_{x}^{\prime}, f_{y}^{\prime},-1\right) \quad\left(\text { 或 } \vec{n}=\left(-f_{x}^{\prime},-f_{y}^{\prime}, 1\right)\right)
$$
