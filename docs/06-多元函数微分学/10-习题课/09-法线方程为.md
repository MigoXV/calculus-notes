## 法线方程为

$$
\frac{x-x_{0}}{F_{x}^{\prime}\left(x_{0}, y_{0}, z_{0}\right)}=\frac{y-y_{0}}{F_{y}^{\prime}\left(x_{0}, y_{0}, z_{0}\right)}=\frac{z-z_{0}}{F_{z}^{\prime}\left(x_{0}, y_{0}, z_{0}\right)}
$$

（2）若曲面方程为（显函数形式）

$$
z=f(x, y)
$$

$\begin{array}{ll}\text { 则可写为隐函数形式 } & F(x, y, z)=0 \\ f(x, y)-z=0 & \text {（或 } \\ f-f(x, y)=0 \text { ）}\end{array}$
曲面上 $M_{0}$ 点的法向量为

$$
\vec{n}=\left(f_{x}^{\prime}, f_{y}^{\prime},-1\right) \quad\left(\text { 或 } \vec{n}=\left(-f_{x}^{\prime},-f_{y}^{\prime}, 1\right)\right)
$$
