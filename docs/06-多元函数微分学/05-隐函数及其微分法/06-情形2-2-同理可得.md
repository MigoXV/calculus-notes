例6．求由方程组 $\left\{\begin{array}{l}z=x^{2}+y^{2} \\ x^{2}+2 y^{2}+3 z^{2}=20\end{array}\right.$ 所确定的隐函数 $z=z(x), y=y(x)$ 的导数 $\frac{d z}{d x}, \frac{d y}{d x}$

解：（公式法）令 $F(x, y, z)=x^{2}+y^{2}-z$
$G(x, y, z)=x^{2}+2 y^{2}+3 z^{2}-20$
$J_{y z}=\frac{\partial(F, G)}{\partial(y, z)}=\left|\begin{array}{ll}F_{y} & F_{z} \\ G_{y} & G_{z}\end{array}\right|=\left|\begin{array}{cc}2 y & -1 \\ 4 y & 6 z\end{array}\right|=12 y z+4 y \neq 0$
$J_{x z}=\frac{\partial(F, G)}{\partial(x, z)}=\left|\begin{array}{cc}F_{x} & F_{z} \\ G_{x} & G_{x}\end{array}\right|=\left|\begin{array}{cc}2 x & -1 \\ 2 x & 6 z\end{array}\right|=12 x z+2 x$
$\therefore \frac{d y}{d x}=-\frac{J_{x z}}{J_{y z}}=-\frac{6 x z+x}{6 y z+2 y}, 2 \frac{d z}{d x}=-\frac{J_{y x}}{J_{y z}}=\frac{x y}{-3 y z+y}-4 x y$

例7 设 $u=u(x, y)$ 由 $\left\{\begin{array}{c}u=f(x, y, z, t) \\ g(y, z, t)=0 \\ h(z, t)=0\end{array}\right.$ 确定，其中 $f, g$ ，h均可微，且 $J=\frac{\partial(g, h)}{\partial(z, t)} \neq 0$ ，求 $\frac{\partial u}{\partial y}$

解：由 $\frac{\partial(g, h)}{\partial(z, t)} \neq 0$ ，知由 $\left\{\begin{array}{c}g(y, z, t)=0 \\ h(z, t)=0\end{array}\right.$ 确定：

$$
\begin{aligned}
& \boldsymbol{z}=\boldsymbol{z}(\boldsymbol{y}), \boldsymbol{t}=\boldsymbol{t}(\boldsymbol{y}) \quad J_{z t}=\frac{\partial(g, h)}{\partial(z, t)}=\left|\begin{array}{ll}
g_{2} & g_{3} \\
h_{1} & h_{2}
\end{array}\right|=g_{2} h_{2}-g_{3} h_{1} \neq 0 \\
& \therefore \frac{d z}{d y}=-\frac{J_{y t}}{J_{z t}}=-\frac{1}{J} g_{1} h_{2}, \frac{d t}{d y}=-\frac{J_{z y}}{J_{z t}}=-\frac{1}{J} g_{1} h_{1} \\
& \therefore \frac{\partial u}{\partial y}=f_{2}+f_{3} \frac{d z}{d y}+f_{4} \frac{d t}{d y}=f_{2}+\frac{1}{J} g_{1}\left(-f_{3} h_{2}+f_{4} h_{1}\right)
\end{aligned}
$$
