\left\{\begin{array}{l}

例1 1．设函数 $z=z(x, y)$ 由方程组 $\left\{\begin{array}{l}x=e^{u+v} \\ y=e^{u-v} \\ z=u v\end{array}\right.$ 所确定，求 $\frac{\partial z}{\partial x}, \frac{\partial z}{\partial y}$
解：$\left\{\begin{array}{l}x=e^{u+v} \\ y=e^{u-v} \\ z=u v\end{array}\right.$ 易知 $\left\{\begin{array}{c}\ln x=u+v \\ \ln y=u-v \\ z=u v\end{array}\right.$ ，即得 $u=\frac{\ln x+\ln y}{2}, v==\frac{\ln x-\ln y}{2}$ ， $z=u v=\frac{1}{4}\left(\ln ^{2} x-\ln ^{2} y\right)$ ，则得：

$$
\begin{aligned}
& \frac{\partial z}{\partial x}=\frac{\ln x}{2 x} \\
& \frac{\partial z}{\partial y}=-\frac{\ln y}{2 y}
\end{aligned}
$$

设 $F(x, y, u, v)=e^{u+v}-x$
$G(x, y, u, v)=e^{u-v}-y$
$\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}=-\frac{J_{x v}}{J_{u v}}=-\frac{\left|\begin{array}{cc}F_{x} & F_{v} \\ G_{x} & G_{v}\end{array}\right|}{\left|\begin{array}{ll}F_{u} & F_{v} \\ G_{u} & G_{v}\end{array}\right|}=\frac{\left|\begin{array}{cc}1 & x \\ 0 & -y\end{array}\right|}{\left|\begin{array}{cc}x & x \\ y & -y\end{array}\right|}=\frac{1}{2 x}$,
$\frac{\partial v}{\partial x}=-\frac{J_{u x}}{J_{u v}}=-\frac{\left|\begin{array}{cc}F_{u} & F_{x} \\ G_{u} & G_{x}\end{array}\right|}{\left|\begin{array}{cc}F_{u} & F_{v} \\ G_{u} & G_{v}\end{array}\right|}=\frac{\left|\begin{array}{cc}x & 1 \\ y & 0\end{array}\right|}{\left|\begin{array}{cc}x & x \\ y & -y\end{array}\right|}=\frac{1}{2 x}$,
同理可得 $\frac{\partial z}{\partial y}=\frac{1}{2 y}, \frac{\partial v}{\partial y}=-\frac{1}{2 y}$

例12．设函数 $x=x(u, v), y=y(u, v)$内有连续的偏导数，且 $\frac{\partial(x, y)}{\partial(u, v)} \neq 0$ 在点 $(u, v)$ 的某一邻域

1）证明函数组 $\left\{\begin{array}{l}x=x(u, v) \\ y=y(u, v)\end{array}\right.$ 在与点 $(u, v)$ 对应的点 $(x, y)$ 的某一邻域内唯一确定一组单值、连续且具有连续偏导数的反函数 $u=u(x, y), v=v(x, y)$ ．

2）求 $u=u(x, y), v=v(x, y)$ 对 $x, y$ 的偏导数．

例12．设函数 $x=x(u, v), y=y(u, v)$ 在点 $(u, v)$ 的某一邻域内有连续的偏导数，且 $\frac{\partial(x, y)}{\partial(u, v)} \neq 0$

1）证明函数组 $\left\{\begin{array}{l}x=x(u, v) \\ y=y(u, v)\end{array}\right.$ 在与点 $(u, v)$ 对应的点 $(x, y)$ 的某一邻域内唯一确定一组单值、连续且具有连续偏导数的反函数 $u=u(x, y), v=v(x, y)$ ．

2）求 $u=u(x, y), v=v(x, y)$ 对 $x, y$ 的偏导数。
解：1）令 $F(x, y, u, v) \equiv x-x(u, v)=0$

$$
G(x, y, u, v) \equiv y-y(u, v)=0
$$

则有

$$
J=\frac{\partial(F, G)}{\partial(u, v)}=\frac{\partial(x, y)}{\partial(u, v)} \neq 0
$$

由定理3可知结论1）成立。
2）求反函数的偏导数．

$$
\left\{\begin{array}{l}
x \equiv x(u(x, y), v(x, y))  \tag{1}\\
y \equiv y(u(x, y), v(x, y))
\end{array}\right.
$$

（1）式两边对 $x$ 求导，得

$$
\left\{\begin{array}{l}
1=\frac{\partial x}{\partial u} \cdot \frac{\partial u}{\partial x}+\frac{\partial x}{\partial v} \cdot \frac{\partial v}{\partial x}  \tag{2}\\
0=\frac{\partial y}{\partial u} \cdot \frac{\partial u}{\partial x}+\frac{\partial y}{\partial v} \cdot \frac{\partial v}{\partial x}
\end{array}\right.
$$

注意 $J \neq 0$ ，从方程组（2）解得

$$
\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}=\frac{1}{J}\left|\begin{array}{ll}
1 & \frac{\partial x}{\partial v} \\
0 & \frac{\partial y}{\partial v}
\end{array}\right|=\frac{1}{J} \frac{\partial y}{\partial v}, \quad \frac{\partial v}{\partial x}=\frac{1}{J}\left|\begin{array}{ll}
\frac{\partial x}{\partial u} & 1 \\
\frac{\partial y}{\partial u} & 0
\end{array}\right|=-\frac{1}{J} \frac{\partial y}{\partial u}
$$

同理，（1）式两边对 $y$ 求导，可得

$$
\frac{\partial u}{\partial y}=-\frac{1}{J} \frac{\partial x}{\partial v}, \quad \frac{\partial v}{\partial y}=\frac{1}{J} \frac{\partial x}{\partial u}
$$

例12的应用：计算极坐标变换 $x=r \cos \theta, y=r \sin \theta$
的反变换的导数。
由于 $\left.J=\frac{\partial(x, y)}{\partial(r, \theta)}=\left|\begin{array}{cc}\cos \theta & -r \sin \theta \\ \sin \theta & r \cos \theta\end{array}\right|=r \quad \right\rvert\, \begin{gathered}\partial x \\ \frac{\partial \theta}{\partial x}=-\frac{1}{J} \frac{\partial y}{\partial r}\end{gathered}$
所以 $\frac{\partial r}{\partial x}=\frac{1}{J} \frac{\partial y}{\partial \theta}=\frac{1}{r} r \cos \theta=\cos \theta=\frac{x}{\sqrt{x^{2}+y^{2}}}$

$$
\frac{\partial \theta}{\partial x}=-\frac{1}{J} \frac{\partial y}{\partial r}=-\frac{1}{r} \sin \theta=-\frac{y}{x^{2}+y^{2}}
$$

同样有 $\frac{\partial r}{\partial y}=\frac{y}{\sqrt{x^{2}+y^{2}}} \quad \frac{\partial \theta}{\partial y}=\frac{x}{x^{2}+y^{2}}$
