定理．设函数 $F(x, y, u, v), G(x, y, u, v)$ 满足：
（1）在点 $P\left(x_{0}, y_{0}, u_{0}, v_{0}\right)$ 的某一邻域内具有连续偏导数；
（2）$F\left(x_{0}, y_{0}, u_{0}, v_{0}\right)=0, G\left(x_{0}, y_{0}, u_{0}, v_{0}\right)=0$ ；
（3）$\left.J\right|_{P}=\left.\frac{\partial(F, G)}{\partial(u, v)}\right|_{P} \neq 0$
则方程组 $F(x, y, u, v)=0, G(x, y, u, v)=0$ 在点 $\left(x_{0}, y_{0}\right)$的某一邻域内可唯一确定一组满足条件 $u_{0}=u\left(x_{0}, y_{0}\right)$ ， $v_{0}=v\left(x_{0}, y_{0}\right)$ 的单值连续函数 $u=u(x, y), v=v(x, y)$ ，且有偏导数公式：

$$
\begin{aligned}
\frac{\partial u}{\partial x} & =-\frac{1}{J} \cdot \frac{\partial(F, G)}{\partial(\underline{x}, v)}=-\frac{1}{\left|\begin{array}{ll}
F_{u} & F_{v} \\
G_{u} & G_{v}
\end{array}\right| \cdot\left|\begin{array}{ll}
F_{x} & F_{v} \\
G_{x} & G_{v}
\end{array}\right|=-\frac{J_{x v}}{J_{u v}}} \\
\frac{\partial u}{\partial y} & =-\frac{1}{J} \frac{\partial(F, G)}{\partial(\underline{y}, v)}=-\frac{1}{\left|\begin{array}{ll}
F_{u} & F_{v} \\
G_{u} & G_{v}
\end{array}\right|}\left|\begin{array}{ll}
F_{y} & F_{v} \\
G_{y} & G_{v}
\end{array}\right|=-\frac{J_{y v}}{J_{u v}} \\
\frac{\partial v}{\partial x} & =-\frac{1}{J} \frac{\partial(F, G)}{\partial(u, \underline{x})}=-\frac{1}{\left|\begin{array}{ll}
F_{u} & F_{v} \\
G_{u} & G_{v}
\end{array}\right|}\left|\begin{array}{ll}
F_{u} & F_{x} \\
G_{u} & G_{x}
\end{array}\right|=-\frac{J_{u x}}{J_{u v}} \\

F_{u} & F_{v} \\
G_{u} & G_{v}

F_{u} & F_{y} \\
G_{u} & C \\

\text { 证明从略, 仅就求导公 } \\

\end{aligned}

例8．设 $x u-y v=0, y u+x v=1$ ，求 $\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}, \frac{\partial u}{\partial y}, \frac{\partial v}{\partial x}, \frac{\partial v}{\partial y}$ ．
解：方程组两边对 $x$ 求导，并移项得

$$
\left\{\begin{array}{c}
x \frac{\partial u}{\partial x}-y \frac{\partial v}{\partial x}=-u \\
y \frac{\partial u}{\partial x}+x \frac{\partial v}{\partial x}=-v
\end{array}\right.
$$

练习：求 $\frac{\partial u}{\partial y}, \frac{\partial v}{\partial y}$
答案：
由题设 $J=\left|\begin{array}{cr}x & -y \\ y & x\end{array}\right|=x^{2}+y^{2} \neq 0$
故有 $\left\{\begin{array}{l}\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}=\frac{1}{J}\left|\begin{array}{cc}-u & -y \\ -v & x\end{array}\right|=-\frac{x u+y v}{x^{2}+y^{2}} \\ \frac{\partial v}{\partial x}=\frac{1}{J}\left|\begin{array}{cc}x & -u \\ y & -v\end{array}\right|=-\frac{x v-y u}{x^{2}+y^{2}}\end{array}\right.$

$$
\left\{\begin{array}{l}
\frac{\partial u}{\partial y}=-\frac{y u-x v}{x^{2}+y^{2}} \\
\frac{\partial v}{\partial y}=-\frac{x u+y v}{x^{2}+y^{2}}
\end{array}\right.
$$

例9 设 $\left\{\begin{array}{l}u=f(u x, v+y) \\ v=g\left(u-x, v^{2} y\right)\end{array}\right.$ ，其中 $f, g$ 具有一阶连续偏导数，求 $\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}, \frac{\partial v}{\partial x}$ ．

解。方程组两边对 $x$ 求偏导得

$$
\begin{aligned}
& \frac{\partial u}{\partial x}=\left(x \frac{\partial u}{\partial x}+u\right) f_{1}^{\prime}+\frac{\partial v}{\partial x} f_{2}^{\prime} \\
& \frac{\partial v}{\partial x}=\left(\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}-1\right) g_{1}^{\prime}+2 y v \frac{\partial v}{\partial x} g_{2}^{\prime}
\end{aligned} \quad \text { 由此可求得 } \frac{\partial u}{\partial \boldsymbol{x}}, \frac{\partial v}{\partial \boldsymbol{x}} \text {. }
$$
