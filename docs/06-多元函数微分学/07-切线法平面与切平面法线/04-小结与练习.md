## 内容小结

1．空间曲线的切线与法平面
1）参数式情况。空间光滑曲线 $\Gamma:\left\{\begin{array}{l}x=\varphi(t) \\ y=\psi(t) \\ z=\omega(t)\end{array}\right.$
切向量 $\quad \vec{T}=\left(\varphi^{\prime}\left(t_{0}\right), \psi^{\prime}\left(t_{0}\right), \omega^{\prime}\left(t_{0}\right)\right)$
切线方程 $\frac{x-x_{0}}{\varphi^{\prime}\left(t_{0}\right)}=\frac{y-y_{0}}{\psi^{\prime}\left(t_{0}\right)}=\frac{z-z_{0}}{\omega^{\prime}\left(t_{0}\right)}$
法平面方程
$\varphi^{\prime}\left(t_{0}\right)\left(x-x_{0}\right)+\psi^{\prime}\left(t_{0}\right)\left(y-y_{0}\right)+\omega^{\prime}\left(t_{0}\right)\left(z-z_{0}\right)=0$

2）一般式情况．空间光滑曲线 $\Gamma:\left\{\begin{array}{l}F(x, y, z)=0 \\ G(x, y, z)=0\end{array}\right.$
切向量 $\quad \vec{T}=\left(\left.\frac{\partial(F, G)}{\partial(y, z)}\right|_{M},\left.\frac{\partial(F, G)}{\partial(z, x)}\right|_{M},\left.\frac{\partial(F, G)}{\partial(x, y)}\right|_{M}\right)$
切线方程

$$
\frac{x-x_{0}}{\left.\frac{\partial(F, G)}{\partial(y, z)}\right|_{M}}=\frac{y-y_{0}}{\left.\frac{\partial(F, G)}{\partial(z, x)}\right|_{M}}=\frac{z-z_{0}}{\left.\frac{\partial(F, G)}{\partial(x, y)}\right|_{M}}
$$

法平面方程 $\left.\quad \frac{\partial(F, G)}{\partial(y, z)}\right|_{M}\left(x-x_{0}\right)+\left.\frac{\partial(F, G)}{\partial(z, x)}\right|_{M}\left(y-y_{0}\right)$

$$
+\left.\frac{\partial(F, G)}{\partial(x, y)}\right|_{M}\left(z-z_{0}\right)=0
$$

## 2.曲面的切平面与法线

1）隐式情况。空间光滑曲面 $\Sigma: F(x, y, z)=0$曲面 $\sum$ 在点 $M\left(x_{0}, y_{0}, z_{0}\right)$ 的法向量

$$
\vec{n}=\left(F_{x}\left(x_{0}, y_{0}, z_{0}\right), F_{y}\left(x_{0}, y_{0}, z_{0}\right), F_{z}\left(x_{0}, y_{0}, z_{0}\right)\right)
$$

切平面方程

$$
\begin{aligned}
F_{x}\left(x_{0}, y_{0}, z_{0}\right)\left(x-x_{0}\right) & +F_{y}\left(x_{0}, y_{0}, z_{0}\right)\left(y-y_{0}\right) \\
& +F_{z}\left(x_{0}, y_{0}, z_{0}\right)\left(z-z_{0}\right)=0
\end{aligned}
$$

法线方程

$$
\frac{x-x_{0}}{F_{x}\left(x_{0}, y_{0}, z_{0}\right)}=\frac{y-y_{0}}{F_{y}\left(x_{0}, y_{0}, z_{0}\right)}=\frac{z-z_{0}}{F_{z}\left(x_{0}, y_{0}, z_{0}\right)}
$$

2）显式情况。空间光滑曲面 $\Sigma: z=f(x, y)$
法向量

$$
\vec{n}=\left(-f_{x},-f_{y}, 1\right)
$$

法线的方向余弦

$$
\begin{aligned}
\cos \alpha=\frac{-f_{x}}{\sqrt{1+f_{x}^{2}+f_{y}^{2}}}, \cos \beta=\frac{-f_{y}}{\sqrt{1+f_{x}^{2}+f_{y}^{2}}} & \\
& \cos \gamma=\frac{1}{\sqrt{1+f_{x}^{2}+f_{y}^{2}}}
\end{aligned}
$$

切平面方程

$$
z-z_{0}=f_{x}\left(x_{0}, y_{0}\right)\left(x-x_{0}\right)+f_{y}\left(x_{0}, y_{0}\right)\left(y-y_{0}\right)
$$

法线方程 $\frac{x-x_{0}}{f_{x}\left(x_{0}, y_{0}\right)}=\frac{y-y_{0}}{f_{y}\left(x_{0}, y_{0}\right)}=\frac{z-z_{0}}{-1}$

求两个圆柱面 $x^{2}+y^{2}=a^{2}, x^{2}+z^{2}=a^{2}$ ，
备用题1
的交线在点 $\left(\frac{a}{\sqrt{2}}, \frac{a}{\sqrt{2}}, \frac{a}{\sqrt{2}}\right)$ 处的切线方程．
解 令 $F(x, y, z)=x^{2}+y^{2}-a^{2}, G(x, y, z)=x^{2}+z^{2}-a^{2}$

$$
M=\left(\frac{a}{\sqrt{2}}, \frac{a}{\sqrt{2}}, \frac{a}{\sqrt{2}}\right)
$$

则

$$
\vec{T}=\left|\begin{array}{ccc}
\vec{i} & \vec{j} & \vec{k} \\
F_{x} & F_{y} & F_{z} \\
G_{x} & G_{y} & G_{z}
\end{array}\right|_{M}=\left|\begin{array}{ccc}
\vec{i} & \vec{j} & \vec{k} \\
2 x & 2 y & 0 \\
2 x & 0 & 2 z
\end{array}\right|_{M}=\left.4(y z,-x z,-x y)\right|_{M}=2 a^{2}(1,-1,-1)
$$

得所求切线方程为

$$
x-\frac{a}{\sqrt{2}}=\frac{y-\frac{a}{\sqrt{2}}}{-1}=\frac{z-\frac{a}{\sqrt{2}}}{-1}
$$
