## 切线方程 $\frac{x-x_{0}}{m}=\frac{y-y_{0}}{n}=\frac{z-z_{0}}{p}$

曲 $\left\{\begin{array}{l}x=x(t) \\ y=y(t) \\ z=z(t)\end{array}\right.$

$$
\vec{T}= \pm\left(x^{\prime}\left(t_{0}\right), y^{\prime}\left(t_{0}\right), z^{\prime}\left(t_{0}\right)\right)
$$

$\stackrel{\text { 方 }}{\text { 程 }}\left\{\begin{array}{l}y=y(x) \\ z=z(x)\end{array}\right.$

$$
\left\{\begin{array}{l}
F(x, y, z)=0 \\
G(x, y, z)=0
\end{array},\right.
$$

$$
\begin{aligned}
\vec{T} & = \pm\left.\left(\left|\begin{array}{ll}
F_{y} & F_{z} \\
G_{y} & G_{z}
\end{array}\right|,\left|\begin{array}{ll}
F_{z} & F_{x} \\
G_{z} & G_{x}
\end{array}\right|,\left|\begin{array}{ll}
F_{x} & F_{y} \\
G_{x} & G_{y}
\end{array}\right|\right)\right|_{p} \\
& = \pm\left\{J_{y z}, J_{z x}, J_{x y}\right\}
\end{aligned}
$$
