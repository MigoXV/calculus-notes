## 法平面方程

$$
\begin{aligned}
\left.\frac{\partial(F, G)}{\partial(y, z)}\right|_{M}\left(x-x_{0}\right) & +\left.\frac{\partial(F, G)}{\partial(z, x)}\right|_{M}\left(y-y_{0}\right) \\
& +\left.\frac{\partial(F, G)}{\partial(x, y)}\right|_{M}\left(z-z_{0}\right)=0
\end{aligned}
$$

也可表为

$$
\left|\begin{array}{ccc}
x-x_{0} & y-y_{0} & z-z_{0} \\
F_{x}(M) & F_{y}(M) & F_{z}(M) \\
G_{x}(M) & G_{y}(M) & G_{z}(M)
\end{array}\right|=0
$$

例 4 求曲线 $x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2}=6, x+y+z=0$ ，在 $(1,-2,1)$ 处的切线及法平面．
解法 1 令 $F=x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2}, G=x+y+z$ ，则

$$
\left.\frac{\partial(F, G)}{\partial(y, z)}\right|_{M}=\left|\begin{array}{cc}
2 y & 2 z \\
1 & 1
\end{array}\right|_{M}=\left.2(y-z)\right|_{M}=-6 ;
$$

$$
\left.\frac{\partial(F, G)}{\partial(z, x)}\right|_{M}=0 ;\left.\quad \frac{\partial(F, G)}{\partial(x, y)}\right|_{M}=6
$$

切向量 $\quad \vec{T}=(-6,0,6)$

切线方程 $\frac{x-1}{-6}=\frac{y+2}{0}=\frac{z-1}{6}$ 即 $\left\{\begin{array}{c}x+z-2=0 \\ y+2=0\end{array}\right.$

法平面方程 $\quad-6 \cdot(x-1)+0 \cdot(y+2)+6 \cdot(z-1)=0$
即 $\quad x-z=0$
解法2 方程组两边对 $x$ 求导，得 $\left\{\begin{array}{c}y \frac{\mathrm{~d} y}{\mathrm{~d} x}+z \frac{\mathrm{~d} z}{\mathrm{~d} x}=-x \\ \frac{\mathrm{~d} y}{\mathrm{~d} x}+\frac{\mathrm{d} z}{\mathrm{~d} x}=-1\end{array}\right.$
解得 $\frac{\mathrm{d} y}{\mathrm{~d} x}=\frac{\left|\begin{array}{cc}-x & z \\ -1 & 1\end{array}\right|}{\left|\begin{array}{cc}y & z \\ 1 & 1\end{array}\right|}=\frac{z-x}{y-z}, \quad \frac{\mathrm{~d} z}{\mathrm{~d} x}=\frac{\left|\begin{array}{cc}y & -x \\ 1 & -1\end{array}\right|}{\left|\begin{array}{cc}y & z \\ 1 & 1\end{array}\right|}=\frac{x-y}{y-z}$
曲线在点 $M(1,-2,1)$ 处有：
切向量 $\vec{T}=\left(1,\left.\frac{\mathrm{~d} y}{\mathrm{~d} x}\right|_{M},\left.\frac{\mathrm{~d} z}{\mathrm{~d} x}\right|_{M}\right)=(1,0,-1)$
