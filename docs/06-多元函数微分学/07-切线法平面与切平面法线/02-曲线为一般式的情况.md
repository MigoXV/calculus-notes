## 2.曲线为一般式的情况

光滑曲线 $\Gamma:\left\{\begin{array}{l}F(x, y, z)=0 \\ G(x, y, z)=0\end{array}\right.$
当 $J=\frac{\partial(F, G)}{\partial(y, z)} \neq 0$ 时，$\Gamma$ 可表示为 $\left\{\begin{array}{l}y=\varphi(x) \\ z=\psi(x)\end{array}\right.$ ，且有

$$
\frac{\mathrm{d} y}{\mathrm{~d} x}=\frac{1}{J} \frac{\partial(F, G)}{\partial(z, x)}, \quad \frac{\mathrm{d} z}{\mathrm{~d} x}=\frac{1}{J} \frac{\partial(F, G)}{\partial(x, y)},
$$

曲线上一点 $M\left(x_{0}, y_{0}, z_{0}\right)$ 处的切向量为

$$
\begin{aligned}
\vec{T} & =\left\{1, \varphi^{\prime}\left(x_{0}\right), \psi^{\prime}\left(x_{0}\right)\right\} \\
& =\left\{1,\left.\frac{1 \partial(F, G)}{J \partial(z, x)}\right|_{M},\left.\frac{1 \partial(F, G)}{J \partial(x, y)}\right|_{M}\right\}
\end{aligned}
$$

或 $\vec{T}=\left\{\left.\frac{\partial(F, G)}{\partial(y, z)}\right|_{M},\left.\frac{\partial(F, G)}{\partial(z, x)}\right|_{M},\left.\frac{\partial(F, G)}{\partial(x, y)}\right|_{M}\right\}$
则在点 $M\left(x_{0}, y_{0}, z_{0}\right)$ 有
切线方程 $\frac{x-x_{0}}{\left.\frac{\partial(F, G)}{\partial(y, z)}\right|_{M}}=\frac{y-y_{0}}{\left.\frac{\partial(F, G)}{\partial(z, x)}\right|_{M}}=\frac{z-z_{0}}{\left.\frac{\partial(F, G)}{\partial(x, y)}\right|_{M}}$
法平面方程 $\left.\frac{\partial(F, G)}{\partial(y, z)}\right|_{M}\left(x-x_{0}\right)+\left.\frac{\partial(F, G)}{\partial(z, x)}\right|_{M}\left(y-y_{0}\right)$
$+\left.\frac{\partial(F, G)}{\partial(x, y)}\right|_{M}\left(z-z_{0}\right)=0$
