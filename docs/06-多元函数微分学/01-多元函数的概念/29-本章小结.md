$$
\lim _{\substack{x \rightarrow 0 \\ y \rightarrow 0}} \frac{x+y}{x-y}=\operatorname{lif}_{\substack{x \rightarrow 0 \\ y \rightarrow 0}} \frac{x \text { 答 } k x}{y}=1+k
