## 此函数定义域不包括 $x, y$ 轴

解：因 $x^{2} y^{2} \leq \frac{1}{4}\left(x^{2}+y^{2}\right)^{2}$ ，令 $r^{2}=x^{2}+y^{2}$ ，则

$$
\left|\frac{1-\cos \left(x^{2}+y^{2}\right)}{\left(x^{2}+y^{2}\right) x^{2} y^{2}}\right| \geq \frac{4\left(1-\cos r^{2}\right)}{r^{6}}
$$

而 $\lim _{r \rightarrow 0} \frac{4\left(1-\cos r^{2}\right)}{r^{6}}=\lim _{r \rightarrow 0} \frac{2 r^{4}}{r^{6}}=\infty$
故 $\quad \lim _{\substack{x \rightarrow 0 \\ y \rightarrow 0}} \frac{1-\cos \left(x^{2}+y^{2}\right)}{\left(x^{2}+y^{2}\right) x^{2} y^{2}}=\infty$

$$
1-\cos r^{2} \sim \frac{r^{2}}{2}
$$
