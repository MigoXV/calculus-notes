## 4.多元复合函数的高阶偏导数习例

例7 设 $u=f(x, y)$ 有二阶连续偏导数，将下列表达式：$\frac{\partial^{2} u}{\partial x^{2}}+\frac{\partial^{2} u}{\partial y^{2}}$ 转化为极坐标形式。

例5 设 $u=f(x+y+z, x y z), f$ 具有二阶连续偏导，求 $\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}, \frac{\partial^{2} u}{\partial x \partial z}$ ．
解 $\quad \frac{\partial u}{\partial x}=\left(f_{1}^{\prime}, f_{2}^{\prime}\right)\binom{1}{y z}=f_{1}^{\prime}+y z f_{2}^{\prime}$
其中 $f_{1}^{\prime}=f_{1}^{\prime}(x+y+z, x y z), f_{2}^{\prime}=f_{2}^{\prime}(x+y+z, x y z)$

$$
\begin{aligned}
\therefore \frac{\partial^{2} u}{\partial x \partial z} & =\left(f_{11}^{\prime \prime}, f_{12}^{\prime \prime}\right)\binom{1}{x y}+y f_{2}^{\prime}+y z\left(f_{21}^{\prime \prime}, f_{22}^{\prime \prime}\right)\binom{1}{x y} \\
& =f_{11}^{\prime \prime}+f_{12}^{\prime \prime} x y+y f_{2}^{\prime}+y z\left(f_{21}^{\prime \prime}+f_{22}^{\prime \prime} x y\right) \\
& =f_{11}^{\prime \prime}+y(x+z) f_{12}^{\prime \prime}+x y^{2} z f_{22}^{\prime \prime}+y f_{2}^{\prime}
\end{aligned}
$$

例6 设 $u=f(x+y+z+x y z), f$ 具有二阶连续导数，求 $\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}, \frac{\partial^{2} u}{\partial x \partial z}$ ．

解 $\quad \frac{\partial u}{\partial x}=f^{\prime} \cdot(1+y z)$
其中 $f^{\prime}=f^{\prime}(x+y+z+x y z)$

$$
\therefore \frac{\partial^{2} u}{\partial x \partial z}=f^{\prime \prime} \cdot(1+x y)(1+y z)+f^{\prime} \cdot y
$$

例7 设 $u=f(x, y)$ 有二阶连续偏导数，将下列表达式：$\frac{\partial^{2} u}{\partial x^{2}}+\frac{\partial^{2} u}{\partial y^{2}}$ 转化为极坐标形式。

解 $\because r=\sqrt{x^{2}+y^{2}}, \theta=\operatorname{arctg} \frac{y}{x}$ ，

$$
\begin{aligned}
& \therefore u=u(r, \theta)=u\left(\sqrt{x^{2}+y^{2}}, \operatorname{arctg} \frac{y}{x}\right) \stackrel{\text { 记为 }}{=} f(x, y) \\
& \begin{aligned}
\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}=\frac{\partial u}{\partial r} \cdot \frac{\partial r}{\partial x}+\frac{\partial u}{\partial \theta} \cdot \frac{\partial \theta}{\partial x} & =\frac{x}{r} \frac{\partial u}{\partial r}-\frac{y}{r^{2}} \frac{\partial u}{\partial \theta} \\
& =\frac{\partial u}{\partial r} \cos \theta-\frac{\partial u}{\partial \theta} \cdot \frac{\sin \theta}{r}
\end{aligned}
\end{aligned}
$$

$$
\begin{aligned}
& \frac{\partial^{2} u}{\partial x^{2}}=\frac{\partial}{\partial r}\left(\frac{\partial u}{\partial r} \cos \theta-\frac{\partial u}{\partial \theta} \cdot \frac{\sin \theta}{r}\right) \frac{\partial r}{\partial x} \\
& +\frac{\partial}{\partial \theta}\left(\frac{\partial u}{\partial r} \cos \theta-\frac{\partial u}{\partial \theta} \cdot \frac{\sin \theta}{r}\right) \frac{\partial \theta}{\partial x} \\
= & \left(\frac{\partial^{2} u}{\partial r^{2}} \cos \theta-\frac{\partial^{2} u}{\partial \theta \partial r} \cdot \frac{\sin \theta}{r}+\frac{\partial u}{\partial \theta} \cdot \frac{\sin \theta}{r^{2}}\right) \cos \theta \\
+ & \left(\frac{\partial^{2} u}{\partial r \partial \theta} \cos \theta-\frac{\partial u}{\partial r} \sin \theta-\frac{\partial^{2} u}{\partial \theta^{2}} \cdot \frac{\sin \theta}{r}-\frac{\partial u}{\partial \theta} \cdot \frac{\cos \theta}{r}\right)\left(-\frac{\sin \theta}{r}\right)
\end{aligned}
$$

$$
=\frac{\partial^{2} u}{\partial r^{2}} \cos ^{2} \theta+\frac{\partial^{2} u}{\partial \theta^{2}} \frac{\sin ^{2} \theta}{r^{2}}+\frac{\partial u}{\partial r} \frac{\sin ^{2} \theta}{r}-\frac{\partial^{2} u}{\partial r \partial \theta} \frac{2 \sin \theta}{r}+\frac{\partial u}{\partial \theta} \frac{\sin 2 \theta}{r^{2}}
$$

同理 $\frac{\partial u}{\partial y}=\frac{\partial u}{\partial r} \cdot \frac{\partial r}{\partial y}+\frac{\partial u}{\partial \theta} \cdot \frac{\partial \theta}{\partial y}=\frac{y}{r} \frac{\partial u}{\partial r}+\frac{x}{r^{2}} \frac{\partial u}{\partial \theta}$

$$
=\frac{\partial u}{\partial r} \sin \theta+\frac{\partial u}{\partial \theta} \frac{\cos \theta}{r}
$$

求得 $\frac{\partial^{2} u}{\partial y^{2}}=\cdots$

$$
\therefore \frac{\partial^{2} u}{\partial x^{2}}+\frac{\partial^{2} u}{\partial y^{2}}=\frac{\partial^{2} u}{\partial r^{2}}+\frac{1}{r} \frac{\partial u}{\partial r}+\frac{1}{r^{2}} \frac{\partial^{2} u}{\partial \theta^{2}}
$$
