## 1.一阶微分形式的不变性

设函数 $z=f(u, v)$ 具有连续偏导数，则有全微分

$$
d z=\frac{\partial z}{\partial u} d u+\frac{\partial z}{\partial v} d v ;
$$

又若 $u, v$ 是 $x, y$ 的函数，即 $u=\varphi(x, y), v=\psi(x, y)$ ，且有连续偏导，则 $z=f[\varphi(x, y), \psi(x, y)]$ 的全微分为

$$
\begin{aligned}
d z & =\frac{\partial z}{\partial x} d x+\frac{\partial z}{\partial y} d y \\
& =\left(\frac{\partial z}{\partial u} \cdot \frac{\partial u}{\partial x}+\frac{\partial z}{\partial v} \cdot \frac{\partial v}{\partial x}\right) d x+\left(\frac{\partial z}{\partial u} \cdot \frac{\partial u}{\partial y}+\frac{\partial z}{\partial v} \cdot \frac{\partial v}{\partial y}\right) d y
\end{aligned}
$$

$$
\begin{aligned}
& =\frac{\partial z}{\partial u}\left(\frac{\partial u}{\partial x} d x+\frac{\partial u}{\partial y} d y\right)+\frac{\partial z}{\partial v}\left(\frac{\partial v}{\partial x} d x+\frac{\partial v}{\partial y} d y\right) \\
& =\frac{\partial z}{\partial u} d u+\frac{\partial z}{\partial v} d v
\end{aligned}
$$

无论 $u, v$ 是自变量还是中间变量，都有

$$
d z=\frac{\partial z}{\partial u} d u+\frac{\partial z}{\partial v} d v
$$

这就是一阶全微分形式不变性。
