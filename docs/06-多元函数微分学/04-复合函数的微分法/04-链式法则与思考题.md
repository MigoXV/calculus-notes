## 1.复合函数求导的链式法则

＂分段用乘，分叉用加，单路全导，叉路偏导＂
例如，$u=f(x, y, v), v=\varphi(x, y)$ ，

$$
\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}=f_{1}^{\prime}+f_{3}^{\prime} \cdot \varphi_{1}^{\prime} ; \quad \frac{\partial u}{\partial y}=f_{2}^{\prime}+f_{3}^{\prime} \cdot \varphi_{2}^{\prime}
$$

## 思考题

1．已知 $\left.f(x, y)\right|_{y=x^{2}}=1,\left.f_{1}^{\prime}(x, y)\right|_{y=x^{2}}=2 x$ ，求 $\left.f_{2}^{\prime}(x, y)\right|_{y=x^{2}}$ ．
解：由 $f\left(x, x^{2}\right)=1$ 两边对 $x$ 求导，得

$$
\begin{gathered}
f_{1}^{\prime}\left(x, x^{2}\right)+f_{2}^{\prime}\left(x, x^{2}\right) \cdot 2 x=0 \\
f_{1}^{\prime}\left(x, x^{2}\right)=2 x \\
f_{2}^{\prime}\left(x, x^{2}\right)=-1
\end{gathered}
$$

2．设函数 $z=f(x, y)$ 在点 $(1,1)$ 处可微，且

$$
f(1,1)=1,\left.\quad \frac{\partial f}{\partial x}\right|_{(1,1)}=2,\left.\quad \frac{\partial f}{\partial y}\right|_{(1,1)}=3,
$$

$\varphi(x)=f(x, f(x, x))$ ，求 $\left.\frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d} x} \varphi^{3}(x)\right|_{x=1}$ 。（2001考研）
解：由题设 $\varphi(1)=f(1, f(1,1))=f(1,1)=1$

$$
\left.\begin{aligned}
& \left.\frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{~d} x} \varphi^{3}(x)\right|_{x=1}=\left.3 \varphi^{2}(x) \frac{\mathrm{d} \varphi}{\mathrm{~d} x}\right|_{x=1} \\
& \quad=3\left[f_{1}^{\prime}(x, f(x, x))\right. \\
& \left.\quad+f_{2}^{\prime}(x, f(x, x))\left(f_{1}^{\prime}(x, x)+f_{2}^{\prime}(x, x)\right)\right] \\
& =3 \cdot[2+3 \cdot(2+3)]=51
\end{aligned}\right|_{x=1}
$$
