## 内容小结

1．微分定义：$(z=f(x, y))$

$$
\begin{gathered}
\Delta z=f_{x}(x, y) \Delta x+f_{y}(x, y) \Delta y+o(\rho) \\
\rho=\sqrt{(\Delta x)^{2}+(\Delta y)^{2}} \\
\mathrm{~d} z=f_{x}(x, y) \mathrm{d} x+f_{y}(x, y) \mathrm{d} y
\end{gathered}
$$

## 3.微分应用

－近似计算

$$
\begin{aligned}
& \Delta z \approx f_{x}(x, y) \Delta x+f_{y}(x, y) \Delta y \\
& f(x+\Delta x, y+\Delta y) \\
& \quad \approx f(x, y)+f_{x}(x, y) \Delta x+f_{y}(x, y) \Delta y
\end{aligned}
$$

## 估计误差

- 绝对误差：$\delta_z=\left|f_x(x,y)\right|\delta_x+\left|f_y(x,y)\right|\delta_y$
- 相对误差：$\frac{\delta_z}{|z|}=\left|\frac{f_x(x,y)}{f(x,y)}\right|\delta_x+\left|\frac{f_y(x,y)}{f(x,y)}\right|\delta_y$
