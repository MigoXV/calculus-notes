## 五、向量的分向量与向量的坐标

1．向量在坐标轴上的分向量与向量的坐标
设 $\vec{a}=\vec{M}_{1} M_{2}$ 为一向量，$u$ 为一条数轴。点 $M_{1}, M_{2}$ 在轴 $u$ 上的投影分别为点 $P_{1}, P_{2}$ ．又设 $P_{1}, P_{2}$ 在轴 $u$ 上的坐标依次为 $u_{1}, u_{2}$ ．

$$
\begin{gathered}
\because \quad \operatorname{Pr} j_{u}{\overrightarrow{M_{1} M_{2}}}=a_{u}, \\
P_{1} P_{2}=O P_{2}-O P_{1} \\
=u_{2}-u_{1}, \\
\therefore \quad a_{u}=u_{2}-u_{1} .
\end{gathered}
$$

如果 $\vec{e}$ 是与 $u$ 轴正向一致的单位向量，由例5知

$$
{\overleftrightarrow{P_{1} P_{2}}}=a_{u} \vec{e}=\left(u_{2}-u_{1}\right) \vec{e} .
$$

设 $\vec{a}$ 是以 $M_{1}\left(x_{1}, y_{1}, z_{1}\right)$ 为起点、 $M_{2}\left(x_{2}, y_{2}, z_{2}\right)$为终点的向量，过 $\boldsymbol{M}_{1}, \boldsymbol{M}_{2}$ 各作垂直于三个坐标轴的平面，这六个平面围成一个以线段 $M_{1} M_{2}$ 为对角线的长方体．

以 $\vec{i}, \vec{j}, \vec{k}$ 分别表示沿 $x, y, z$ 轴正向的单位向量．

$$
\begin{gathered}
a_{x}=x_{2}-x_{1} \quad \text { 崩 } \quad \text { 崩 } \\
a_{y}=y_{2}-y_{1} \quad a_{z}=z_{2}-z_{1} \quad \text { 投 影 } \\
\quad \vec{M}_{1} M_{2}=\left(x_{2}-x_{1}\right) \vec{i}+\left(y_{2}-y_{1}\right) \vec{j}+\left(z_{2}-z_{1}\right) \vec{k}
\end{gathered}
$$

$$
\vec{a}=a_{x} \vec{i}+a_{y} \vec{j}+a_{z} \vec{k}
$$

按基本单位向量的坐标分解式：

$$
\overrightarrow{M_{1} M_{2}}=\left(x_{2}-x_{1}\right) \vec{i}+\left(y_{2}-y_{1}\right) \vec{j}+\left(z_{2}-z_{1}\right) \vec{k}
$$

在三个坐标轴上的分向量：$a_{x} \vec{i}, a_{y} \vec{j}, a_{z} \vec{k}$ ，向量的坐标：$a_{x}, a_{y}, a_{z}$ ，向量的坐标表达式：$\vec{a}=\left\{a_{x}, a_{y}, a_{z}\right\}$

$$
\overrightarrow{M_{1} M_{2}}=\left\{x_{2}-x_{1}, y_{2}-y_{1}, z_{2}-z_{1}\right\}
$$

特殊地： $\overrightarrow{O M}=\{x, y, z\}$

2．向量的加减法、向量与数的乘法运算的坐标表达式

$$
\begin{aligned}
& \vec{a}=\left\{a_{x}, a_{y}, a_{z}\right\}, \quad \vec{b}=\left\{b_{x}, b_{y}, b_{z}\right\} \\
& \begin{aligned}
\vec{a}+\vec{b} & =\left\{a_{x}+b_{x}, a_{y}+b_{y}, a_{z}+b_{z}\right\} \\
& =\left(a_{x}+b_{x}\right) \vec{i}+\left(a_{y}+b_{y}\right) \vec{j}+\left(a_{z}+b_{z}\right) \vec{k} ;
\end{aligned} \\
& \begin{aligned}
\vec{a}-\vec{b} & =\left\{a_{x}-b_{x}, a_{y}-b_{y}, a_{z}-b_{z}\right\} \\
& =\left(a_{x}-b_{x}\right) \vec{i}+\left(a_{y}-b_{y}\right) \vec{j}+\left(a_{z}-b_{z}\right) \vec{k} ;
\end{aligned} \\
& \begin{aligned}
\lambda \vec{a}= & \left\{\lambda a_{x}, \lambda a_{y}, \lambda a_{z}\right\} \\
= & \left(\lambda a_{x}\right) \vec{i}+\left(\lambda a_{y}\right) \vec{j}+\left(\lambda a_{z}\right) \vec{k} .
\end{aligned}
\end{aligned}
$$

例6．设 $A\left(x_{1}, y_{1}, z_{1}\right)$ 和 $B\left(x_{2}, y_{2}, z_{2}\right)$ 为两已知点，而在 $\boldsymbol{A} \boldsymbol{B}$ 直线上的点 $\boldsymbol{M}$ 分有向线段 $\boldsymbol{A} \boldsymbol{B}$ 为两部分 $A M 、 M B$ ，使它们的值的比等于某数 $\lambda(\lambda \neq-1)$ ，即 $\frac{\overrightarrow{A M}}{\overrightarrow{M B}}=\lambda$ ，求分点的坐标．

解 设 $M(x, y, z)$ 为直线上的点，

$$
\begin{aligned}
& \overrightarrow{A M}=\left\{x-x_{1}, y-y_{1}, z-z_{1}\right\} \\
& \overrightarrow{M B}=\left\{x_{2}-x, y_{2}-y, z_{2}-z\right\}
\end{aligned}
$$

由题意知： $\overrightarrow{A M}=\lambda \overrightarrow{M B}$

$$
\begin{aligned}
&\left\{x-x_{1}, y-y_{1}, z-z_{1}\right\}=\lambda\left\{x_{2}-x, y_{2}-y, z_{2}-z\right\} \\
& x-x_{1}=\lambda\left(x_{2}-x\right) \Rightarrow x=\frac{x_{1}+\lambda x_{2}}{1+\lambda} \\
& y-y_{1}=\lambda\left(y_{2}-y\right) \Rightarrow y=\frac{y_{1}+\lambda y_{2}}{1+\lambda} \\
& z-z_{1}=\lambda\left(z_{2}-z\right) \Rightarrow z=\frac{z_{1}+\lambda z_{2}}{1+\lambda}
\end{aligned}
$$

$\boldsymbol{M}$ 为有向线段 $\boldsymbol{A B}$ 的定比分点． $\boldsymbol{M}$ 为中点时，

$$
x=\frac{x_{1}+x_{2}}{2}, \quad y=\frac{y_{1}+y_{2}}{2}, \quad z=\frac{z_{1}+z_{2}}{2} \text {. }
$$
