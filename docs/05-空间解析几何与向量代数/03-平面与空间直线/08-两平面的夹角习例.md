例6 研究以下各组里两平面的位置关系：
（1）$-x+2 y-z+1=0, \quad y+3 z-1=0$
（2） $2 x-y+z-1=0, \quad-4 x+2 y-2 z-1=0$
（3） $2 x-y-z+1=0, \quad-4 x+2 y+2 z-2=0$
解（1） $\cos \theta=\frac{|-\mathbf{1} \times \mathbf{0}+\mathbf{2} \times \mathbf{1}-\mathbf{1} \times \mathbf{3}|}{\sqrt{(\mathbf{- 1})^{\mathbf{2}}+\mathbf{2}^{\mathbf{2}}+(\mathbf{- 1})^{\mathbf{2}}} \cdot \sqrt{\mathbf{1}^{\mathbf{2}}+\mathbf{3}^{\mathbf{2}}}} \cos \theta=\frac{1}{\sqrt{60}}$ 两平面相交，夹角 $\theta=\arccos \frac{1}{\sqrt{60}}$ ．
（2）$\vec{n}_{1}=\{2,-1,1\}, \quad \vec{n}_{2}=\{-4,2,-2\}$
$\Rightarrow \frac{2}{-4}=\frac{-1}{2}=\frac{1}{-2}$ ，两平面平行
$\because M(\mathbf{1}, \mathbf{1}, \mathbf{0}) \in \Pi_{1} \quad M(\mathbf{1}, \mathbf{1}, \mathbf{0}) \notin \Pi_{2}$
两平面平行但不重合。
（3）$\because \frac{2}{-4}=\frac{-1}{2}=\frac{-1}{2}=\frac{D_{1}}{D_{2}}$ ，
∴ 两平面重合．

例7 设 $P_{0}\left(x_{0}, y_{0}, z_{0}\right)$ 是平面 $A x+B y+C z+D=0$外一点，求 $P_{0}$ 到平面的距离．

解 $\forall P_{1}\left(x_{1}, y_{1}, z_{1}\right) \in \Pi$

$$
\begin{gathered}
d=\left|\operatorname{Pr} j_{n} \overrightarrow{P_{1} P_{0}}\right| \\
\operatorname{Pr} j_{n} \overrightarrow{P_{1} P_{0}}=\overrightarrow{P_{1} P_{0}} \cdot \overrightarrow{n^{0}} \\
\overrightarrow{P_{1} P_{0}}=\left\{x_{0}-x_{1}, y_{0}-y_{1}, z_{0}-z_{1}\right\}
\end{gathered}
$$

$$
\begin{aligned}
& \overrightarrow{n^{0}}=\left\{\frac{A}{\sqrt{A^{2}+B^{2}+C^{2}}}, \frac{B}{\sqrt{A^{2}+B^{2}+C^{2}}}, \frac{C}{\sqrt{A^{2}+B^{2}+C^{2}}}\right\} \\
& \therefore \operatorname{Pr} j_{n} \overrightarrow{P_{1} P_{0}}=\overrightarrow{P_{1} P_{0}} \cdot \overrightarrow{n^{0}} \\
& =\frac{A\left(x_{0}-x_{1}\right)}{\sqrt{A^{2}+B^{2}+C^{2}}}+\frac{B\left(y_{0}-y_{1}\right)}{\sqrt{A^{2}+B^{2}+C^{2}}}+\frac{C\left(z_{0}-z_{1}\right)}{\sqrt{A^{2}+B^{2}+C^{2}}} \\
& =\frac{A x_{0}+B y_{0}+C z_{0}-\left(A x_{1}+B y_{1}+C z_{1}\right)}{\sqrt{A^{2}+B^{2}+C^{2}}}
\end{aligned}
$$

$$
\begin{aligned}
& \because A x_{1}+B y_{1}+C z_{1}+D=0 \quad\left(P_{1} \in \Pi\right) \\
& \therefore \operatorname{Pr} j_{n} \overrightarrow{P_{1} P_{0}}=\frac{A x_{0}+B y_{0}+C z_{0}+D}{\sqrt{A^{2}+B^{2}+C^{2}}} \\
& \therefore d=\frac{\left|A x_{0}+B y_{0}+C z_{0}+D\right|}{\sqrt{A^{2}+B^{2}+C^{2}}} .
\end{aligned}
$$
