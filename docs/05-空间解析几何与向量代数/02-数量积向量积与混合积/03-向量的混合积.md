## 三、向量的混合积

1．定义 已知三向量 $\vec{a}, \vec{b}, \vec{c}$ ，称数量

$$
(\vec{a} \times \vec{b}) \cdot \vec{c} \xlongequal{\text { 记作 }}(\vec{a}, \vec{b}, \vec{c})
$$

为 $\vec{a}, \vec{b}, \vec{c}$ 的混合积。

## 几何意义

以 $\vec{a}, \vec{b}, \vec{c}$ 为棱作平行六面体，则其

底面积 $A=|\vec{a} \times \vec{b}|$ ，高 $h=|\vec{c}||\cos \alpha|$故平行六面体体积为

$$
\begin{aligned}
V & =A h=|\vec{a} \times \vec{b}||\vec{c}||\cos \alpha|=|(\vec{a} \times \vec{b}) \cdot \vec{c}| \\
& =|(\vec{a}, \vec{b}, \vec{c})|
\end{aligned}
$$

## 2.混合积的坐标表示

设 $\vec{a}=\left(a_{x}, a_{y}, a_{z}\right), \vec{b}=\left(b_{x}, b_{y}, b_{z}\right), \vec{c}=\left(c_{x}, c_{y}, c_{z}\right)$

$$
\begin{aligned}
& \vec{a} \times \vec{b}=\left|\begin{array}{ccc}
\vec{i} & \vec{j} & \vec{k} \\
a_{x} & a_{y} & a_{z} \\
b_{x} & b_{y} & b_{z}
\end{array}\right|=\left|\begin{array}{cc}
a_{y} & a_{z} \\
b_{y} & b_{z}
\end{array}\right| \vec{i}-\left|\begin{array}{cc}
a_{x} & a_{z} \\
b_{x} & b_{z}
\end{array}\right| \vec{j}+\left|\begin{array}{cc}
a_{x} & a_{y} \\
b_{x} & b_{y}
\end{array}\right| \vec{k} \\
&(\vec{a}, \vec{b}, \vec{c})=(\vec{a} \times \vec{b}) \cdot \vec{c}=\left|\begin{array}{cc}
a_{y} & a_{z} \\
b_{y} & b_{z}
\end{array}\right| c_{x}-\left|\begin{array}{cc}
a_{x} & a_{z} \\
b_{x} & b_{z}
\end{array}\right| c_{y}+\left|\begin{array}{cc}
a_{x} & a_{y} \\
b_{x} & b_{y}
\end{array}\right| c_{z} \\
&=\left|\begin{array}{ccc}
a_{x} & a_{y} & a_{z} \\
b_{x} & b_{y} & b_{z} \\
c_{x} & c_{y} & c_{z}
\end{array}\right| \\
&=\left|\begin{array}{ccc}
c_{x} & c_{y} & c_{z} \\
a_{x} & a_{y} & a_{z} \\
b_{x} & b_{y} & b_{z}
\end{array}\right|
\end{aligned}
$$

## 3.混合积的性质

（1）三个非零向量 $\vec{a}, \vec{b}, \vec{c}$ 共面的充要条件是

$$
(\vec{a}, \vec{b}, \vec{c})=0
$$

（2）轮换对称性：

$$
(\vec{a}, \vec{b}, \vec{c})=(\vec{b}, \vec{c}, \vec{a})=(\vec{c}, \vec{a}, \vec{b})
$$

（可用三阶行列式推出）
保持三矢的循环次序（逆时针），× 号可以与•号互换而混合积不变。

例10 已知一四面体的顶点 $A_{k}\left(x_{k}, y_{k}, z_{k}\right)(k=1,2,3,4)$求该四面体体积．

例11 证明四点 $A(\mathbf{1}, \mathbf{1}, \mathbf{1}), B(\mathbf{4}, \mathbf{5}, \mathbf{6}), C(\mathbf{2}, \mathbf{3}, \mathbf{3})$ ， $D(10,15,17)$ 共面．

例9 已知 $(\overrightarrow{\boldsymbol{a}} \overrightarrow{\boldsymbol{b}} \overrightarrow{\boldsymbol{c}})=\mathbf{2}$ ，
计算 $[(\vec{a}+\vec{b}) \times(\vec{b}+\vec{c})] \cdot(\vec{c}+\vec{a})$.
解 $[(\vec{a}+\vec{b}) \times(\vec{b}+\vec{c})] \cdot(\vec{c}+\vec{a})$

$$
\begin{aligned}
& =[\vec{a} \times \vec{b}+\vec{a} \times \vec{c}+\vec{b} \times \vec{b}+\vec{b} \times \vec{c})] \cdot(\vec{c}+\vec{a}) \\
& =(\vec{a} \times \vec{b}) \cdot \vec{c}+\underbrace{(\vec{a} \times \vec{c}) \cdot \vec{c}}_{=0}+\overrightarrow{0} \cdot \vec{c}+\underbrace{(\vec{b} \times \vec{c}) \cdot \vec{c}}_{=0} \\
& \quad+\underset{0}{(\vec{a} \times \vec{b}) \cdot \vec{a}}+\underset{=0}{(\vec{a} \times \vec{c}) \cdot \vec{a}}+\overrightarrow{0} \cdot \vec{a}+\underset{=0}{(\vec{b} \times \vec{c}) \cdot \vec{a}} \\
& =2(\vec{a} \times \vec{b}) \cdot \vec{c}=2(\vec{a}, \vec{b}, \vec{c})=4 .
\end{aligned}
$$

例10 已知一四面体的顶点 $A_{k}\left(x_{k}, y_{k}, z_{k}\right)(k=1,2,3,4)$求该四面体体积．

解 已知四面体的体积等于以向量 $\vec{A}_{1} A_{2}, \vec{A}_{1} A_{3}, \vec{A}_{1} A_{4}$为棱的平行六面体体积的 $\frac{1}{6}$ ，故

$$
\begin{aligned}
V & =\frac{1}{6}\left|\left(\overrightarrow{A_{1} A_{2}}, \overrightarrow{A_{1} A_{3}}, \overrightarrow{A_{1} A_{4}}\right)\right| \\
& =\frac{1}{6}| | \begin{array}{lll}
x_{2}-x_{1} & y_{2}-y_{1} & z_{2}-z_{1} \\
x_{3}-x_{1} & y_{3}-y_{1} & z_{3}-z_{1} \\
x_{4}-x_{1} & y_{4}-y_{1} & z_{4}-z_{1}
\end{array}| |
\end{aligned}
$$

例11 证明四点 $A(1,1,1), B(4,5,6), C(2,3,3)$ ， $D(10,15,17)$ 共面．

解 $\because(\overrightarrow{A B}, \overrightarrow{A C}, \overrightarrow{A D})$

$$
=\left|\begin{array}{rrr}
3 & 4 & 5 \\
1 & 2 & 2 \\
9 & 14 & 16
\end{array}\right|=0
$$

故 $A, B, C, D$ 四点共面．
