## 2.1 导数及微分

## 一、对数求导法

方法：先在方程两边取对数，然后利用隐函数的求导方法求出导数。

适用范围：多个函数相乘和幂指函数 $u(x)^{v(x)}$ 的情形。
设 $y=u(x)^{v(x)}$ ，求 $\frac{d y}{d x}$ ．
解：首先，两边取对数 $\ln y=v(x) \ln u(x)$ ，
两边求导 $\frac{1}{y} \cdot y^{\prime}=v^{\prime}(x) \ln u(x)+v(x) \cdot \frac{u^{\prime}(x)}{u(x)}$ ，
$\therefore y^{\prime}=u(x)^{v(x)}\left[v^{\prime}(x) \ln u(x)+v(x) \cdot \frac{u^{\prime}(x)}{u(x)}\right]$ ．

例1．设 $y=\frac{(x+1) \sqrt[3]{x-1}}{(x+4)^{2} e^{x}}$ ，求 $y^{\prime}$ ．
解：等式两边取对数得

$$
\ln y=\ln (x+1)+\frac{1}{3} \ln (x-1)-2 \ln (x+4)-x
$$

上式两边对 $x$ 求导得

$$
\begin{aligned}
& \frac{y^{\prime}}{y}=\frac{1}{x+1}+\frac{1}{3(x-1)}-\frac{2}{x+4}-1 \\
& \therefore y^{\prime}=\frac{(x+1) \sqrt[3]{x-1}}{(x+4)^{2} e^{x}}\left[\frac{1}{x+1}+\frac{1}{3(x-1)}-\frac{2}{x+4}-1\right]
\end{aligned}
$$

例 2．设 $y=x^{\sin x}(x>0)$ ，求 $y^{\prime}$ ．
解：等式两边取对数得 $\ln y=\sin x \cdot \ln x$
上式两边对 $x$ 求导得

$$
\begin{aligned}
\frac{1}{y} y^{\prime} & =\cos x \cdot \ln x+\sin x \cdot \frac{1}{x} \\
\therefore y^{\prime} & =y\left(\cos x \cdot \ln x+\sin x \cdot \frac{1}{x}\right) \\
& =x^{\sin x}\left(\cos x \cdot \ln x+\frac{\sin x}{x}\right)
\end{aligned}
$$

例3．设 $y=\left(\frac{a}{b}\right)^{x}\left(\frac{b}{x}\right)^{a}\left(\frac{x}{a}\right)^{b}\left(a>0, b>0, \frac{a}{b} \neq 1\right)$ ，求 $y^{\prime}$ ．
解：$y=\left(\frac{a}{b}\right)^{x}\left(\frac{b}{x}\right)^{a}\left(\frac{x}{a}\right)^{b}\left(a>0, b>0, \frac{a}{b} \neq 1\right)$

例4．设 $y=\left(x^{2}+1\right)^{\sin x}$ ，求 $y^{\prime}$ ．
解：$\because \ln y=\sin x \cdot \ln \left(x^{2}+1\right)$ ，

$$
\begin{aligned}
& \frac{d}{d x}(\ln y)=\frac{d}{d x}\left[\sin x \cdot \ln \left(x^{2}+1\right)\right] \\
& \frac{1}{y} \cdot y^{\prime}=\cos x \cdot \ln \left(x^{2}+1\right)+\sin x \cdot \frac{2 x}{x^{2}+1} \\
\therefore & y^{\prime}=\left(x^{2}+1\right)^{\sin x}\left[\cos x \cdot \ln \left(x^{2}+1\right)+\frac{2 x \sin x}{x^{2}+1}\right] .
\end{aligned}
$$

例5．设 $y=\frac{5}{\sqrt{\frac{x-5}{x^{2}+2}}}$ ，求 $y^{\prime}$ ．
解：$\because \ln y=\frac{1}{5}\left[\ln (x-5)-\frac{1}{5} \ln \left(x^{2}+2\right)\right]$ ，

$$
\begin{aligned}
& \frac{d}{d x}(\ln y)=\frac{d}{d x}\left\{\frac{1}{5}\left[\ln (x-5)-\frac{1}{5} \ln \left(x^{2}+2\right)\right]\right\}, \\
& \frac{1}{y} \cdot y^{\prime}=\frac{1}{5}\left(\frac{1}{x-5}-\frac{1}{5} \frac{2 x}{x^{2}+2}\right), \\
\therefore & y^{\prime}=\frac{1}{25} \sqrt[5]{\frac{x-5}{\sqrt[5]{x^{2}+2}}}\left(\frac{5}{x-5}-\frac{2 x}{x^{2}+2}\right) .

$$

三、由参数方程确定的函数的求导法则

$$
\text { 1. }\left\{\begin{array} { l } 
{ x = \varphi ( t ) } \\
{ y = \psi ( t ) }
\end{array} \rightarrow \left\{\begin{array}{l}
\text { a. } y=f(x) \rightarrow \frac{d y}{d x}=f^{\prime}(x) \\
\text { b. } F(x, y)=0 \rightarrow \frac{d y}{d x}=f^{\prime}(x)
\end{array}\right.\right.
$$

2．问题：消参困难或无法消参时如何求导？
定理：设 $\left\{\begin{array}{l}x=\varphi(t) \\ y=\psi(t)\end{array}\right.$ 确定了 $y$ 为 $x$ 的函数，
$x=\varphi(t)$ 和 $y=\psi(t)$ 可导，且 $\varphi^{\prime}(t) \neq 0$,
则 $\frac{d y}{d x}=\frac{\psi^{\prime}(t)}{\varphi^{\prime}(t)}$ ．

## 证明：方法 1.利用导数的定义．

$\because x=\varphi(t)$ 可导，$\therefore x=\varphi(t)$ 连续。
从而当 $\Delta x \rightarrow 0$ 时 $\Delta t \rightarrow 0$ ．

$$
\begin{aligned}
& \lim _{\Delta x \rightarrow 0} \frac{\Delta y}{\Delta x}=\lim _{\Delta t \rightarrow 0} \frac{\Delta y}{\Delta x}=\lim _{\Delta t \rightarrow 0} \frac{\psi(t+\Delta t)-\psi(t)}{\varphi(t+\Delta t)-\varphi(t)} \\
& \quad=\lim _{\Delta t \rightarrow 0} \frac{\psi(t+\Delta t)-\psi(t)}{\frac{\Delta t}{\varphi(t+\Delta t)-\varphi(t)}}=\frac{\psi^{\prime}(t)}{\Delta t}, \therefore \frac{d y}{\varphi^{\prime}(t)}=\frac{\psi^{\prime}(t)}{\varphi^{\prime}(t)} .
\end{aligned}
$$

方法 2．利用复合函数的求导法则，有

$$
\begin{equation*}
\frac{d y}{d x}=\frac{d y}{d t} \frac{d t}{d x}=\frac{\frac{d y}{d t}}{\frac{d x}{d t}}=\frac{\psi^{\prime}(t)}{\varphi^{\prime}(t)} \tag{回}
\end{equation*}
$$

## 一般来说有如下说明：

若参数方程 $\left\{\begin{array}{l}x=\varphi(t) \\ y=\psi(t)\end{array}\right.$ 可确定一个 $\boldsymbol{y}$ 与 $\boldsymbol{x}$ 之间的函数关系，$\varphi(t), \psi(t)$ 可导，且 $\left[\varphi^{\prime}(t)\right]^{2}+\left[\psi^{\prime}(t)\right]^{2} \neq 0$ ，则 $\varphi^{\prime}(t) \neq 0$ 时，有

$$
\frac{\mathrm{d} y}{\mathrm{~d} x}=\frac{\mathrm{d} y}{\mathrm{~d} t} \cdot \frac{\mathrm{~d} t}{\mathrm{~d} x}=\frac{\mathrm{d} y}{\mathrm{~d} t} \cdot \frac{1}{\frac{\mathrm{~d} x}{\mathrm{~d} t}}=\frac{\psi^{\prime}(t)}{\varphi^{\prime}(t)}
$$

$\psi^{\prime}(t) \neq 0$ 时，有

$$
\frac{\mathrm{d} x}{\mathrm{~d} y}=\frac{\mathrm{d} x}{\mathrm{~d} t} \cdot \frac{\mathrm{~d} t}{\mathrm{~d} y}=\frac{\mathrm{d} x}{\mathrm{~d} t} \cdot \frac{1}{\mathrm{~d} y}=\frac{\varphi^{\prime}(t)}{\psi^{\prime}(t)}
$$

（此时看成 $\boldsymbol{x}$ 是 $\boldsymbol{y}$ 的函数） $\mathrm{d} t$

若上述参数方程中 $\varphi(t), \psi(t)$ 二阶可导，且 $\varphi^{\prime}(t) \neq 0$ ，则由它确定的函数 $y=f(x)$ 可求二阶导数。

利用新的参数方程 $\left\{\begin{array}{l}x=\varphi(t) \\ \frac{\mathrm{d} y}{\mathrm{~d} x}=\frac{\psi^{\prime}(t)}{\varphi^{\prime}(t)}\end{array}\right.$, 可得

$$
\begin{aligned}
\frac{\mathrm{d}^{2} y}{\mathrm{~d} x^{2}} & =\frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{~d} x}\left(\frac{\mathrm{~d} y}{\mathrm{~d} x}\right)=\frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{~d} t}\left(\frac{\mathrm{~d} y}{\mathrm{~d} x}\right) / \frac{\mathrm{d} x}{\mathrm{~d} t} \\
& =\frac{\psi^{\prime \prime}(t) \varphi^{\prime}(t)-\psi^{\prime}(t) \varphi^{\prime \prime}(t)}{\varphi^{\prime 2}(t)} / \varphi^{\prime}(t) \\
& =\frac{\psi^{\prime \prime}(t) \varphi^{\prime}(t)-\psi^{\prime}(t) \varphi^{\prime \prime}(t)}{\varphi^{\prime 3}(t)}=\frac{\boldsymbol{y}^{\prime \prime} \boldsymbol{x}^{\prime}-\boldsymbol{x}^{\prime \prime} \boldsymbol{y}^{\prime}}{\boldsymbol{x}_{(\mathrm{a})}^{\prime 3}}
\end{aligned}
$$

例6．设 $\left\{\begin{array}{l}x=\ln \left(1+t^{2}\right) \\ y=t-\arctan t\end{array}\right.$, 求 $\frac{d^{3} y}{d x^{3}}$.
解：$\because \frac{d y}{d x}=\frac{\frac{d y}{d t}}{d x}=\frac{1-\frac{1}{1+t^{2}}}{2 t}=\frac{t}{2}$ ，

$$
\begin{align*}
& \frac{d^{2} y}{d x^{2}}=\frac{\frac{d}{d t}\left(\frac{d t}{2}\right)}{\frac{d x}{d t}}=\frac{\frac{1}{2}}{\frac{1+t^{2}}{1+t^{2}}}=\frac{1+t^{2}}{4 t}, \\
& \frac{d^{3} y}{d x^{3}}=\frac{\frac{d}{d t}\left(\frac{1+t^{2}}{4 t}\right)}{\frac{d x}{d t}}=\frac{\frac{2 t \cdot 4 t-\left(1+t^{2}\right) 4}{16 t^{2}}}{\frac{2 t}{1+t^{2}}}=\frac{t^{4}-1}{8 t^{3}} . \tag{8}
\end{align*}
$$

例7．设由方程 $\left\{\begin{array}{l}x=t^{2}+2 t \\ t^{2}-y+\varepsilon \sin y=1\end{array} \quad(0<\varepsilon<1)\right.$确定函数 $y=y(x)$ ，求 $\frac{\mathrm{d} y}{\mathrm{~d} x}$ ．

解：方程组两边对 $t$ 求导，得

$$
\left\{\begin{array} { l } 
{ \frac { \mathrm { d } x } { \mathrm { d } t } = 2 t + 2 } \\
{ 2 t - \frac { \mathrm { d } y } { \mathrm { d } t } + \varepsilon \operatorname { c o s } y \frac { \mathrm { d } y } { \mathrm { d } t } = 0 }
\end{array} \Longrightarrow \left\{\begin{array}{l}
\frac{\mathrm{d} x}{\mathrm{~d} t}=2(t+1) \\
\frac{\mathrm{d} y}{\mathrm{~d} t}=\frac{2 t}{1-\varepsilon \cos y}
\end{array}\right.\right.
$$

故 $\quad \frac{\mathrm{d} y}{\mathrm{~d} x}=\frac{\mathrm{d} y}{\mathrm{~d} t} / \frac{\mathrm{d} x}{\mathrm{~d} t}=\frac{t}{(t+1)(1-\varepsilon \cos y)}$ ．

例8．设 $\left\{\begin{array}{l}t+x-\cos y=0 \\ x-t e^{x}-1=0\end{array}\right.$ ，求 $\frac{d^{2} y}{d x^{2}}$ ．
解：因为 $\boldsymbol{x}$ 和 $\boldsymbol{y}$ 都是关于 $\boldsymbol{t}$ 的可微函数，则

$$
\begin{aligned}
& \left\{\begin{array}{l}
1+\frac{d x}{d t}+\sin y \cdot \frac{d y}{d t}=0 \\
\frac{d x}{d t}-e^{x}-t e^{x} \frac{d x}{d t}=0
\end{array}\right. \\
& \frac{d x}{d t}=\frac{e^{x}}{1-t e^{x}}=\frac{e^{x}}{2-x} \\
& \frac{d y}{d t}=\frac{t e^{x}-e^{x}-1}{\sin y \cdot\left(1-t e^{x}\right)}=\frac{x-e^{x}-2}{(2-x) \sin y}
\end{aligned}
$$

$$
\begin{aligned}
& \therefore \frac{d y}{d x}=\frac{\frac{d y}{d t}}{\frac{d t}{d t}}=\frac{x-e^{x}-2}{e^{x} \sin y} \\
& \text { 从而 } \frac{d^{2} y}{d x^{2}}=\left(\frac{x-e^{x}-2}{e^{x} \sin y}\right)^{\prime} \\
& =\frac{\left(1-e^{x}\right) e^{x} \sin y-\left(x-e^{x}-2\right)\left(e^{x} \sin y+e^{x} \cos y \cdot \frac{d y}{d x}\right)}{\left(e^{x} \sin y\right)^{2}} \\
& =\cdots=\frac{(3-x) e^{x} \sin ^{2} y-\left(x-e^{x}-2\right)^{2} \cos y}{e^{2 x} \sin ^{3} y}
\end{aligned}
$$

例9．抛射体运动轨迹的参数方程为 $\left\{\begin{array}{l}x=v_{1} t \\ y=v_{2} t-\frac{1}{2} g t^{2}\end{array}\right.$求抛射体在时刻 $t$ 的运动速度的大小和方向．

解：先求速度大小
速度的水平分量为 $\frac{\mathrm{d} x}{\mathrm{~d} t}=v_{1}$ ，垂直分量为 $\frac{\mathrm{d} y}{\mathrm{~d} t}=v_{2}-g t$ ，故抛射体速度大小

$$
v=\sqrt{\left(\frac{\mathrm{d} x}{\mathrm{~d} t}\right)^{2}+\left(\frac{\mathrm{d} y}{\mathrm{~d} t}\right)^{2}}=\sqrt{v_{1}^{2}+\left(v_{2}-g t\right)^{2}} .
$$

再求速度方向（即轨迹的切线方向）：设 $\alpha$ 为切线倾角，则

$$
\tan \alpha=\frac{\mathrm{d} y}{\mathrm{~d} x}=\frac{\mathrm{d} y}{\mathrm{~d} t} / \frac{\mathrm{d} x}{\mathrm{~d} t}=\frac{v_{2}-g t}{v_{1}} .
$$

抛射体轨迹的参数方程 $\left\{\begin{array}{l}x=v_{1} t \\ y=v_{2} t-\frac{1}{2} g t^{2}\end{array}\right.$
速度的水平分量 $\frac{\mathrm{d} x}{\mathrm{~d} t}=v_{1}$ ，垂直分量 $\frac{\mathrm{d} y}{\mathrm{~d} t}=v_{2}-g t$ ，速度的方向 $\tan \alpha=\frac{v_{2}-g t}{v_{1}}$
在刚射出（即 $\boldsymbol{t}=\mathbf{0}$ ）时，倾角为

$$
\alpha=\arctan \frac{v_{2}}{v_{1}}
$$

达到最高点的时刻 $t=\frac{v_{2}}{g}$ ，高度 $\left.y\right|_{t=\frac{v_{2}}{g}}=\frac{1}{2} \frac{v_{2}^{2}}{g}$
落地时刻 $t=\frac{2 v_{2}}{g}$ ，抛射最远距离 $\left.x\right|_{t=\frac{2 v_{2}}{g}}=\frac{2 v_{1} v_{2}}{g^{\circ}(A)}$

## 五、分段函数的求导法

分段函数求导的不同之处是分段点处的导数必须用定义求．
（1）设 $G(x)=\left\{\begin{array}{ll}f(x) & x \neq x_{0} \\ a & x=x_{0}\end{array}\right.$ ，求 $G^{\prime}(x)$ 。
解：当 $x \neq x_{0}$ 时，$G^{\prime}(x)=f^{\prime}(x)$ ，
当 $x=x_{0}$ 时，$G^{\prime}\left(x_{0}\right)=\lim _{x \rightarrow x_{0}} \frac{G(x)-G\left(x_{0}\right)}{x-x_{0}}=\lim _{x \rightarrow x_{0}} \frac{f(x)-a}{x-x_{0}}$ ，
注意：$G^{\prime}\left(x_{0}\right) \neq(a)^{\prime}$
（2）设 $H(x)=\left\{\begin{array}{ll}f(x) & x \leq x_{0} \\ g(x) & x>x_{0}\end{array}, ~\right.$ 求 $H^{\prime}(x)$ 。
解：当 $x<x_{0}$ 时，$H^{\prime}(x)=f^{\prime}(x)$

当 $x>x_{0}$ 时，$H^{\prime}(x)=g^{\prime}(x)$
当 $x=x_{0}$ 时，

$$
\begin{aligned}
& H_{-}^{\prime}\left(x_{0}\right)=\lim _{x \rightarrow x_{0}^{-}} \frac{H(x)-H\left(x_{0}\right)}{x-x_{0}}=\lim _{x \rightarrow x_{0}^{-}} \frac{f(x)-f\left(x_{0}\right)}{x-x_{0}}, \\
& H_{+}^{\prime}\left(x_{0}\right)=\lim _{x \rightarrow x_{0}^{+}} \frac{H(x)-H\left(x_{0}\right)}{x-x_{0}}=\lim _{x \rightarrow x_{0}^{+}} \frac{g(x)-f\left(x_{0}\right)}{x-x_{0}},
\end{aligned}
$$

若 $H_{-}^{\prime}\left(x_{0}\right)=H_{+}^{\prime}\left(x_{0}\right)$ ，则 $H^{\prime}\left(x_{0}\right)$ 存在。
若 $H_{-}^{\prime}\left(x_{0}\right) \neq H_{+}^{\prime}\left(x_{0}\right)$ ，则 $H^{\prime}\left(x_{0}\right)$ 不存在。
注意：$H_{-}^{\prime}\left(x_{0}\right) \neq\left. f^{\prime}(x)\right|_{x=x_{0}} \quad H_{+}^{\prime}\left(x_{0}\right) \neq\left. g^{\prime}(x)\right|_{x=x_{0}}$

例10．设 $f(x)=\left\{\begin{array}{ll}\varphi(x) \sin \frac{1}{x} & x \neq 0 \\ 0 & x=0\end{array}\right.$ 求 $\varphi(0)=0, \varphi^{\prime}(0)=0$ ，
解：$\because \varphi(0)=0, \varphi^{\prime}(0)=0$ ，

$$
\begin{aligned}
& \varphi^{\prime}(0)=\lim _{x \rightarrow 0} \frac{\varphi(x)-\varphi(0)}{x-0}=\lim _{x \rightarrow 0} \frac{\varphi(x)}{x}, \\
& \therefore \lim _{x \rightarrow 0} \frac{\varphi(x)}{x}=0,
\end{aligned}
$$

故 $f^{\prime}(0)=\lim _{x \rightarrow 0} \frac{f(x)-f(0)}{x-0}=\lim _{x \rightarrow 0} \frac{\varphi(x) \sin \frac{1}{x}}{x}$

$$
=\lim _{x \rightarrow 0} \frac{\varphi(x)}{x} \cdot \sin \frac{1}{x}=0 .
$$
